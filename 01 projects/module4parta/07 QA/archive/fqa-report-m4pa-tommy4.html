Claims without hedging (Analysis/Evaluation risk)

Several statements on Slide 3 are written as fact rather than evidence-based hypothesis:

  • "HPA axis calibrated to hypervigilance" — should be "associated with hypervigilant stress responses"
  • "Increased amygdala reduces PFC function" — should be "associated with amygdala hyperreactivity that can override PFC function"
  • "IWM: adults = unpredictable; safety = fragile" — this is Tommy's likely IWM, not a verified fact

Adding hedging language ("consistent with," "associated with," "the evidence suggests") demonstrates the methodological awareness the A+ descriptor requires.
