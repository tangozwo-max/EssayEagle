## Aspects of recovery—Fay, DJ, and Andy

There are many different ways of going through recovery, and people have different stories and different aspects that are important for them. On this page, we will look at the stories of three people: Fay, DJ, and Andy. Each of them tells their story and talks about the aspect of recovery that is important to them.

These videos are from an organization in New Hampshire in the USA called The Doorway. The organization seeks to provide holistic help and support to people with substance use disorders. Their centers are set up so that no one is more than an hour away from help. There is also a 24-hour chat line.
