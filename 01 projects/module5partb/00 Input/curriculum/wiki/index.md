- [[brief-interventions]] — NICE guidance; 5–60 min; primary care; ASSIST-BI
- [[contingency-management]] — Vouchers/cash for abstinence; strong short-term evidence
- [[12-step-programs]] — AA/NA/TSF; Cochrane: higher abstinence; spirituality critique
- [[relapse-prevention]] — CBT-based trigger identification, coping skills, crisis planning
- [[creative-arts-therapies]] — Music, art, exercise, yoga, acupuncture as SUD adjuncts
- [[medically-assisted-withdrawal]] — MAW/detox; alcohol vs opioid severity; inpatient/community
- [[opioid-substitution-therapy]] — OST overview; methadone vs buprenorphine vs lofexidine
- [[methadone]] — Full mu agonist + NMDA antagonist; long half-life; accumulation risk
- [[buprenorphine]] — Partial mu agonist; ceiling effect; Subutex/Suboxone
- [[lofexidine]] — Alpha-2 agonist; non-opioid; second-line withdrawal management
- [[acamprosate]] — NMDA/GABA balance restoration; abstinence maintenance in AUD
- [[disulfiram]] — Aldehyde dehydrogenase inhibitor; aversion therapy for AUD
- [[nalmefene]] — Pan-opioid antagonist; harm reduction goal in AUD; as-needed dosing
- [[baclofen]] — GABA-B agonist; suppresses alcohol dopamine reward; off-label AUD
- [[future-pharmacological-interventions]] — Psychedelics, kudzu, bupropion, cocaine pharmacotherapy
