# ASAM — American Society of Addictive Medicine

A US professional medical society representing physicians and other healthcare professionals who specialize in addiction medicine. ASAM is influential in defining and disseminating clinical definitions of addiction.

## Key definition (cited in M5)
"Addiction is a treatable, chronic medical disease involving complex interactions among brain circuits, genetics, the environment, and an individual's life experiences. People with addiction use substances or engage in behaviors that become compulsive and often continue despite harmful consequences."

This definition is significant because it:
- Explicitly includes behavioral addiction (not just substance use)
- Frames addiction as a medical disease, not a moral failing
- Highlights the complexity of neurobiological, genetic, environmental, and experiential interactions
