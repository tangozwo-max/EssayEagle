# Cochrane

International not-for-profit organisation producing high-quality systematic reviews. **CGMH** (established 2017) covers schizophrenia, common mental-health conditions, dementia, developmental/psychosocial/learning conditions, drugs/alcohol. Focuses on LMIC-relevant evidence synthesis.
