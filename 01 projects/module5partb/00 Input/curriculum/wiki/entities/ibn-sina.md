# Ibn Sina (Avicenna)

Father of Modern Medicine. Wrote the **Canon of Medicine** (standard medical textbook for 500+ years). Described mania, hallucinations, dementia, melancholia, schizophrenia; named the cerebellar vermis and caudate nucleus.
