# APA — American Psychiatric Association

The largest psychiatric organization in the world, responsible for publishing the Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders ([[dsm]]). The APA defines compulsion as "a type of behavior or a mental act engaged in to reduce anxiety or distress."

## Key DSM-5 changes relevant to M5
- Replaced "substance abuse" and "substance dependence" with the unified term **[[substance-use-disorder]]** (2013)
- Introduced a severity continuum (mild/moderate/severe) based on number of diagnostic criteria met
- Recognized gambling as a behavioral addiction (only behavioral addiction in DSM-5)
- Removed the term "addiction" from DSM-5; severe SUD = colloquial "addiction"

## Note on APA acronym overlap
The American *Psychological* Association also uses the acronym APA. In M5 W1, APA in the context of DSM and psychiatric diagnoses refers to the American *Psychiatric* Association.
