# MAC-UK

MAC-UK is a UK organisation that exemplifies community psychology in action. It takes mental health support out of traditional clinical settings into community environments, working with socially excluded youth. Its INTEGRATE model co-produces mental health interventions with young people and local stakeholders. MAC-UK employs individuals with lived experience and draws on evidence-based psychological practices to create sustainable change, addressing root causes of mental ill-health (social exclusion, inequality, economic and health policies).

## Key contributions
- INTEGRATE model — co-production of community-based mental health for excluded youth
- Employment of people with lived experience in service delivery
