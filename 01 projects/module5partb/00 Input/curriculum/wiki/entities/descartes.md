# René Descartes (1596–1650)

French philosopher and mathematician. Proposed the most well-known version of **mind-body dualism** in the 17th century (Cartesian dualism).

## Key contributions
- Body = extended substance (measurable, divisible)
- Mind = unextended/immaterial (consciousness, thoughts, experience)
- Acknowledged mind-body interaction but admitted the mechanism was mysterious

## Impact on medicine
- Separated body as an object of scientific inquiry from the soul
- Enabled anatomy, physiology, and the biomedical model
- Isolated mental health from medicine for 200+ years
