# John Burnham

John Burnham is a British systemic therapist and clinical psychologist, best known for developing the Social GRACES model with colleagues. The Social GRACES framework helps practitioners and service users reflect on the dimensions of social identity that influence therapeutic relationships and clinical practice. His work emphasises reflexivity and cultural sensitivity in systemic therapy.

## Key contributions
- Social GRACES model — framework for reflecting on identity and social positioning in therapy
- Cultural reflexivity in systemic practice
