# World Health Organization (WHO)

UN specialised agency for international public health. Central authority on global mental-health policy, frameworks, and guidelines referenced throughout the curriculum.

## Key WHO contributions relevant to this course
- [[csdh-framework]] (2008, 2024 renewal) — Commission on Social Determinants of Health
- **mhGAP** — Mental Health Gap Action Program (see [[mhgap-five-keys]])
- Operational frameworks for measuring and addressing SDH inequities
- Data and reports on HIC/LMIC mental-health workforce gaps
- Prevention-levels taxonomy (primary, secondary, tertiary)

## In M5 context — Substance Use
- Defines [[psychoactive-substance]] as substances that alter consciousness, mood, or thinking
- Defines [[dependence-syndrome]] (ICD-10/ICD-11): binary diagnosis (present/absent), 6 criteria
- Mental health features of acute intoxication table (by substance class)
- Global Status Report (2024): 400M adults with alcohol use disorders; 209M with alcohol dependence
- Reports on substance use treatment coverage gaps globally (20% of countries lack screening programs)
