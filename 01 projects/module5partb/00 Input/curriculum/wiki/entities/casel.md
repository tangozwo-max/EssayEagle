# CASEL

CASEL (Collaborative for Academic, Social, and Emotional Learning) is a US-based not-for-profit, multidisciplinary network dedicated to establishing high-quality, evidence-based social and emotional learning (SEL) in schools. In 2011, CASEL launched the Collaborating Districts Initiative (CDI) for systematic SEL implementation in large urban districts across the USA. After 10 years, districts demonstrated sustained commitment to SEL even through leadership changes, with new schools joining and SEL expanding to all staff roles (including security and bus drivers).

## Key contributions
- Collaborating Districts Initiative (CDI) — systematic SEL implementation model
- Evidence base for sustained school-wide SEL
