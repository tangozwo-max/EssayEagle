# Camillo Golgi (1843–1926)

Italian physician and biologist. Developed the silver-nitrate staining technique (1873) — the Golgi stain — that enabled the study of individual neurons. Endorsed reticular theory (incorrect); Nobel 1906 alongside Cajal.
