# Carl Rogers (1902–1987)

American psychologist. Founder of **person-centered (client-centered) therapy**. One of the most influential figures in humanistic psychology.

## Core contributions
- Three necessary and sufficient conditions for therapeutic change:
  1. **Unconditional positive regard** — accept the client without judgment
  2. **Empathic understanding** — genuinely understand the client's perspective
  3. **Congruence** — therapist's authenticity, including body language

## Influence
- Non-directive supportive counselling
- Person-centered care in healthcare (beyond psychotherapy)
- Motivational Interviewing (influenced by Rogers)
