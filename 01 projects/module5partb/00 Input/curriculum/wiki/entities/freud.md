# Sigmund Freud (1856–1939)

Austrian neurologist and founder of psychoanalysis. Proposed the unconscious, the Oedipus complex, defence mechanisms, and the structural model (id, ego, superego). Case study of **Little Hans** (1909) influential in early 20th-century psychology.
