# Melanie Klein

Melanie Klein (1882–1960) was an Austrian-British psychoanalyst who pioneered object relations theory and psychoanalytic work with children. She developed play analysis — using children's play as the equivalent of free association in adults — allowing access to unconscious processes in young clients. Her work on early anxiety, defense mechanisms, and the role of fantasy significantly advanced psychodynamic child therapy.

## Key contributions
- Object relations theory — emotional development through relationships with internal objects
- Play analysis — using toys and play as therapeutic medium
- Work on infant mental life, paranoid-schizoid and depressive positions
