# M4 W5 P71 — School Behavior Policies

School behavior policies outline expected standards, incident procedures, and consequences including detention, suspension, and exclusion. Problems: uneven application, punishment-focus over positive reinforcement, disproportionate impact on vulnerable groups (minorities, disabled students), failure to account for SEN, poverty, or mental health. 2024 As I Am report (*What We Wish You Knew*) found Irish behavior codes are implicitly ableist, rewarding neurotypical norms and punishing neurodivergent behavior patterns.
