# M4 W1 P12 — Cognitive development

This page details cognitive development across infancy, toddlerhood, preschool, childhood, and adolescence. It covers object permanence and symbolic thinking in infancy, egocentric and magical thinking in preschool, concrete operational thinking in childhood (ages 5-11), and formal operational thinking in adolescence. The page illustrates how cognitive abilities progress from action-based, concrete understanding to abstract, logical reasoning.
