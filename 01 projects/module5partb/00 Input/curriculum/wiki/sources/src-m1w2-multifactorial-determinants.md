# M1 W2 — The Multifactorial Determinants of Mental Health

## Overview
Explores the biological, psychological, and social determinants of mental health, and the frameworks that describe how they interact. Introduces distal vs proximal factors, the Dahlgren-Whitehead model, the WHO CSDH framework, the Lund et al. five-domain systematic review, and the implications for prevention and multidisciplinary care.

## Learning outcomes
- Describe the key multifactorial determinants of mental health.
- Explain distal and proximal factors.
- Evaluate and critique the core socioeconomic models.
- Examine interplay of biological, psychological, social determinants.
- Explain roles of members of a multidisciplinary mental health team.
- Describe interventions for multifactorial challenges.
