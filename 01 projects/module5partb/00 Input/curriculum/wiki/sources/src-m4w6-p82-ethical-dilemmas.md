# M4 W6 P82 — Ethical Dilemmas in CAMH

Introduces ethical considerations specific to young people in CAMH: confidentiality and disclosure, informed consent, autonomy/capacity/competency, best interests of the child, mandatory reporting, and cultural/social sensitivity. These ethical challenges vary significantly by setting and country. Prior ethics content from M1 W3 is cross-referenced.

## Key concepts covered
- [[confidentiality-camh]] — balancing disclosure obligations with child's right to privacy
- [[informed-consent]] — consent in CAMH; developmental stage and capacity considerations
- [[mandatory-reporting]] — legal obligation to report child protection concerns
- [[best-interests-child]] — legal/ethical principle guiding clinical decisions about children
