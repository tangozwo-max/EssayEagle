# M4 W1 P15 — COVID-19 and Developmental Milestones

Impact of COVID-19 on developmental milestones. US, Japan and UK studies: modest but real delays in speech, socio-emotional development, new separation anxiety. Teen brains showed ~3 years accelerated ageing.

## Key concepts covered
- [[developmental-milestones]] - pandemic caused delays across domains
- [[separation-anxiety]] - new onset in older children returning to school
- [[adverse-childhood-experiences]] - pandemic as population-level ACE
