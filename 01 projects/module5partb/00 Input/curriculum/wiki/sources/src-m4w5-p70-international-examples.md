# M4 W5 P70 — International Examples of School Mental Health

Two international case studies. Kazakhstan AMHSP: government/UNICEF/Bilim Foundation program using digital monitoring to identify at-risk adolescents; trained 116,000 school personnel; 10,000+ referrals; reduced suicidal ideation, depression, anxiety. CASEL (USA): Collaborating Districts Initiative for systematic SEL in large urban districts since 2011; sustained implementation attributed to strong leadership, core district policies, customisable pathways, staff culture, co-creation with communities, and communities of practice.

- [[sel-programs]] — Social and Emotional Learning; sustained by leadership, policy, community co-creation
