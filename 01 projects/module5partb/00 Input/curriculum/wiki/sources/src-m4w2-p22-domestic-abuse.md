# M4 W2 P22 — Domestic abuse as a risk factor

Domestic abuse serves as both an independent risk factor for infant/child mental health and a driver of maternal depression. Effects vary by developmental stage: prenatal exposure increases miscarriage and low birth weight risk; infants/toddlers experience toxic stress affecting attachment; adolescents face emotional dysregulation, social difficulties, and increased risk of substance use and self-harm.
