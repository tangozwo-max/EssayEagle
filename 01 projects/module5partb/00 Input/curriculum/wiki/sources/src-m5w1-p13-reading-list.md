# M5 W1 P13 — Reading List: Addiction and Dependency — Concepts and Risk Factors

Mandatory reading list for M5 W1.

## Required readings

1. **DSM-5 Criteria for Substance Use Disorders: Recommendations and Rationale**
   URL: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3767415/
   Topic: Rationale for DSM-5 changes from abuse/dependence to SUD framework

2. **Substance-Related and Addictive Disorders** (APA)
   URL: https://www.psychiatry.org/file%20library/psychiatrists/practice/dsm/apa_dsm-5-substance-use-disorder.pdf
   Topic: Full APA clinical overview of DSM-5 substance-related disorders

3. **The dose-response relationship between socioeconomic deprivation and alcohol-attributable mortality risk — systematic review and meta-analysis** (Probst et al.)
   URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s12916-021-02132-z
   Topic: SES and alcohol mortality; education as strongest indicator

4. **Self-management with alcohol over lifespan: psychological mechanisms, neurobiological underpinnings, and risk assessment**
   URL: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC10615763/
   Topic: Lifespan perspective on alcohol self-management and psychological risk factors

5. **Emotion regulation in substance use disorders: a systematic review and meta-analysis**
   URL: https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/add.16001
   Topic: Emotion regulation deficits in SUD

6. **Impulsivity as a vulnerability marker for substance-use disorders**
   URL: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0740547214000269
   Topic: Impulsivity as cross-cutting psychological risk factor
