# M4 W2 P26 — Neurodevelopmental conditions (NDCs) and mental health risk

Introduces terminology distinguishing neurodiversity (differences in brain functioning across everyone), neurodivergence (established cognitive differences without specified cause or onset), and neurodevelopmental conditions (diagnosed disorders with known/potential cause and developmental onset). NDCs represent a potential mental health determinant.

## Key concepts covered
- [[neurodiversity]] — natural brain variation across all humans
- [[neurodivergence]] — established cognitive differences
- [[neurodevelopmental-conditions]] — diagnosed developmental disorders
- [[neurotypical]] — typical brain development and functioning
