# M4 W1 P13 — Emotional development

This page describes emotional development across infancy, toddlerhood, preschool, childhood, and adolescence. It covers infants' early emotional regulation strategies and caregiver dependence, the emergence of self-conscious emotions in toddlers, increasing emotional control in preschool and childhood, and the intensified emotional experiences and meta-emotions of adolescence. The page introduces the concept of "storm and stress" as characterising adolescent emotional development.
