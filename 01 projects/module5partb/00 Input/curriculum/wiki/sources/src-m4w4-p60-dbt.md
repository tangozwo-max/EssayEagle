# M4 W4 P60 — Dialectical Behavioral Therapy

DBT targets self-harm, treatment interfering behaviours, and quality-of-life issues through four core skills: mindfulness, interpersonal effectiveness, emotion regulation, and distress tolerance. DBT-A (adapted for adolescents) typically includes weekly individual sessions, family sessions, and phone support. Originally designed for adults with BPD, its application to adolescents is controversial because personality is still developing. Resource-intensive; simpler skills-only programs are not equivalent to full DBT-A.
