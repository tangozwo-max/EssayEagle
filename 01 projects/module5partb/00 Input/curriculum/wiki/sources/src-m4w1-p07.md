# M4 W1 P07 — Supporting brain development

This page describes strategies for supporting healthy brain development, emphasising secure attachment, play, and attunement. It introduces the NSPCC's framework of brain-building metaphors: brain architecture, serve-and-return, stress management, look-say-sing-play, and air traffic control (executive function). The page discusses the importance of positive stress versus toxic stress and provides practical guidance for caregivers.
