# M4 W2 P21 — Maternal depression as a familial risk factor

Perinatal depression (antenatal and postnatal) represents a significant risk factor for infant and child development. The page summarizes evidence linking maternal depression to poor physical and neurocognitive outcomes, with risk factors including intimate partner violence, low socioeconomic status, and lack of social support.
