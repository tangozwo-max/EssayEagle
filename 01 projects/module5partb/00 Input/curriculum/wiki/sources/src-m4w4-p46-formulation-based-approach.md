# M4 W4 P46 — A Formulation-Based Approach to Mental Health

This page describes the standard clinical process in CAMH: assessment (clinical interviews, questionnaires, observations, neuropsychological tests), formulation (holistic sense-making), intervention (tailored plan including therapy, medication, school accommodations), and evaluation (ongoing monitoring). The 5Ps model of case formulation—Presenting difficulties, Precipitating factors, Protective factors, Perpetuating factors, Predisposing factors—is introduced as the core CBT-derived framework.

## Key concepts covered
- [[clinical-approach-camh]] — four-stage process: assess, formulate, intervene, evaluate
- [[5ps-formulation]] — structured CBT framework for understanding mental health presentations
- [[diagnosis-vs-formulation]] — distinction between diagnostic labelling and holistic sense-making
