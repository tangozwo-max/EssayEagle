# M4 W5 P74 — Global Examples of Community Psychology for Youth Mental Health

Case studies: MAC-UK (UK) uses the INTEGRATE model—co-producing mental health interventions with young people outside clinical settings, employing those with lived experience. Jigsaw (Ireland, 2006): 13 centres, youth aged 12–25, Youth Advisory Panel, significant decrease in mental distress (n=2,420). NJR (Netherlands): youth advocacy for stigma reduction, inspired by Headspace. CHAT (Singapore, 2009): youth aged 16–30; 73.9% of referrals self-initiated; CHAT Ambassadors since 2014. Headspace (Australia, 2006): 100+ centres, high user satisfaction, 50% report reduced distress.
