# M4 W1 P04 — Attachment and mental health services

This page examines how attachment theory applies to mental health service design and delivery. It explores how attachment experiences and styles influence service users' engagement with healthcare providers and services, discusses the role of practitioners as attachment figures, and identifies service design challenges that undermine secure attachment-based care.
