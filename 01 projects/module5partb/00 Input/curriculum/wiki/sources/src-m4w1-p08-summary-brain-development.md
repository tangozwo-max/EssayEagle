# M4 W1 P08 — Summary for Brain Development in the Early Years

Summary: 90% of brain volume by age 6; PFC matures around age 25. Secure attachment supports play and learning. Executive function and caregiver strategies.

- [[executive-function]] - mental skills for task management and problem-solving
- [[secure-attachment]] - fosters environment for play and learning
- [[neuroplasticity]] - brain capacity to change in response to experience
