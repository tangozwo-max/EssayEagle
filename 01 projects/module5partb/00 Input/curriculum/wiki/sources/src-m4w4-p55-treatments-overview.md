# M4 W4 P55 — Treatments for Children and Adolescents

Introduces the main psychological therapies used in CAMH: CBT (thought-behavior change), psychodynamic therapy (unconscious processes, past experiences), systemic family therapy (family dynamics and relationships), and dialectical behavioral therapy (emotion regulation, mindfulness). Treatment plans are individualized and often combine psychotherapy, medication, and lifestyle changes. The stepped-care model guides treatment selection and escalation.
