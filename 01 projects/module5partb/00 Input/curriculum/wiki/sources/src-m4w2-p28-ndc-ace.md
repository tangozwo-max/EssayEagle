# M4 W2 P28 — Neurodevelopmental conditions and adverse childhood experiences

Introduces the "double jeopardy" concept: children with both NDCs and ACEs face compounded risk for poor mental health outcomes. Maltreated children are 10 times more likely to have multiple NDCs. ACEs can delay diagnosis of autism and ADHD. Clinicians should conduct full neurodevelopmental assessment in maltreated children.

## Key concepts covered
- [[double-jeopardy]] — compounded risk from NDC + ACE exposure
- [[child-maltreatment]] — abuse and neglect
- [[diagnostic-delay]] — delayed identification of NDCs due to trauma
- [[neurodevelopmental-assessment]] — comprehensive evaluation
