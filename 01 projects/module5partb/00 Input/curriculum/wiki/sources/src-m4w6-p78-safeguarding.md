# M4 W6 P78 — Safeguarding

Safeguarding protects children from abuse, harm, and neglect while upholding their human rights. Types of abuse include physical, sexual, emotional, neglect, domestic abuse, CSE, cyberbullying, FGM, trafficking, criminal exploitation, and radicalisation. Children from Black, Asian, and minoritised ethnic communities face additional risks due to unconscious bias, adultification (seeing them as more mature and less vulnerable), overlooked protection concerns, and overrepresentation in the youth justice system. Practitioners must use strength-based approaches and build trusting relationships.
