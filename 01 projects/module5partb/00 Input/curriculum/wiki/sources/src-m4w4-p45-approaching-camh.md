# M4 W4 P45 — Approaching Child and Adolescent Mental Health

This introductory page for M4 W4 frames child and adolescent mental health (CAMH) as requiring a systemic, trauma-informed, and neuroaffirmative perspective. It emphasises that young people's mental health is embedded in family, school, and service systems, and that professionals must remain curious rather than dismissive. Services must be developmentally appropriate and accessible.

## Key concepts covered
- [[clinical-approach-camh]] — assessment, formulation, intervention, evaluation for young people
- [[multidisciplinary-team]] — MDT collaboration across health, education, and social care
- [[neuroaffirmative-approach]] — affirming neurological diversity in services
- [[trauma-informed-care]] — creating safe, non-retraumatising environments
