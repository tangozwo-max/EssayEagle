# M4 W2 P31 — Trauma and mental health risk

Examines trauma terminology and impacts on child mental health. Introduces DSM-5 trauma definition (exposure to death, serious injury, sexual violence), PTSD diagnostic criteria (re-experiencing, avoidance, hyperarousal), and concept of intergenerational trauma.

## Key concepts covered
- [[trauma]] — overwhelming distressing events with lasting negative impact
- [[ptsd]] — post-traumatic stress disorder diagnosis
- [[intergenerational-trauma]] — trauma effects passing across generations
- [[re-experiencing]] — intrusive trauma memories
- [[hyperarousal]] — heightened threat responsiveness
- [[avoidance]] — emotional/behavioral avoidance of trauma reminders
