# M4 W4 P51 — Anxiety in Adolescents

Anxiety disorders affect approximately 6.5% of children globally (1 in 12 in the USA). Youth-specific presentations include separation anxiety disorder and selective mutism. Emotionally based school avoidance (EBSA) is a developing concept capturing anxiety-driven non-attendance, which has doubled post-COVID-19. Autistic young people are at particularly elevated risk of EBSA (~40% have an anxiety disorder). CBT is the evidence-based treatment of choice; SSRIs effective short-to-medium term but lack long-term evidence.
