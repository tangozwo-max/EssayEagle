# M4 W1 P02 — Attachment theory

This page introduces John Bowlby's foundational attachment theory, covering the definition of attachment as lasting psychological connectedness and its evolutionary basis. The page details Bowlby's four stages of attachment development (pre-attachment, attachment-in-the-making, clear-cut attachment, and reciprocal relationships), characteristics of secure attachment, and patterns of insecure attachment.
