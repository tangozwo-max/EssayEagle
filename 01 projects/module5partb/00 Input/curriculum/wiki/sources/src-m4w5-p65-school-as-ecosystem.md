# M4 W5 P65 — School as an Ecosystem

Applies Bronfenbrenner's ecological systems model to schools: microsystems (child-teacher, child-class, child-family), mesosystems (parent-teacher meetings; parental separation affecting school), exosystems (social structures influencing development indirectly), macrosystems (culture, government policy), and chronosystems (developmental timing of events). Team in the school includes educational psychologists, speech and language therapists, SLT (with designated safeguarding lead), SENDCOs, and school nurses. Team Around the School (TAS) includes CAMHS, social services, charities, mental health practitioners, and healthcare providers.
