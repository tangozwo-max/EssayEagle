# M4 W4 P52 — Self-Harm and Suicidality

Non-suicidal self-injury (NSSI) affects 17.7% of adolescents (21.4% female, 13.7% male). A prior suicide attempt is the strongest predictor of future suicide. Key risk factor categories: psychiatric/psychological (conduct disorder, personality disorders, depression/anxiety), adverse life events (abuse, bullying), behavioral (substance use), and social (poor family relationships, being in foster care). Foster care carries a pooled odds ratio of 3.89 for self-harm. Evidence for psychosocial interventions is limited; DBT-A shows promise.
