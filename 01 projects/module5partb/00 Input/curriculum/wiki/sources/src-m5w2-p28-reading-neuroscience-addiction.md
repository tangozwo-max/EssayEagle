# M5 W2 P28 — Reading: Understanding the Neuroscience of Addiction and Dependency

Required reading list for M5 Week 2. Six resources are assigned covering the neuroscience of addiction, impulsivity/compulsivity, and substance-specific reviews.

## Required readings
1. [Drugs, Brains, and Behavior: The Science of Addiction](https://nida.nih.gov/research-topics/addiction-science/drugs-brain-behavior-science-of-addiction) — NIDA (foundational overview)
2. [Impulsivity and compulsivity](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a_kRRfQTks8) — video lecture
3. [Cannabis Addiction and the Brain: a Review](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC6223748/) — PMC review
4. [Benzodiazepine use, misuse, and abuse: A review](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC6007645/) — PMC review (Schmitz)
5. [The Neurobiology of Cocaine Addiction](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2851032/) — PMC review
6. [Can brains bounce back? Recovering addicts turn to science to map the effects of meth](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2023/may/06/can-brains-bounce-back-recovering-addicts-turn-to-science-to-map-the-effects-of-meth) — The Guardian
