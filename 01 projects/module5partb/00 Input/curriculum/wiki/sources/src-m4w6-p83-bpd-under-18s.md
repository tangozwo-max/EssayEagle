# M4 W6 P83 — Borderline Personality Disorder (BPD) in Under 18s

BPD symptoms include emotional instability, altered thinking/perception, impulsive behaviour, and unstable interpersonal relationships. The debate around diagnosing BPD in under-18s: advocates for early diagnosis argue it enables understanding and early intervention; critics note questions about validity, difficulty confirming pervasiveness in adolescents, normal adolescent identity development, and risk of self-stigma. The diagnosis is also controversial due to its association with trauma history, insufficient attention to social context, and historical misogynistic critiques.
