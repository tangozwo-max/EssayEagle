# M4 W6 P77 — Risk Assessment and Safeguarding (Introduction)

Uses a real case study (Daniel) to illustrate the consequences of failure to share information across agencies — school, healthcare, and social services each had partial knowledge of risk. Key message: safeguarding is everyone's business. Multi-agency coordination and proactive assertive approach is essential; most child protection failures stem from poor information sharing.

- [[safeguarding]] — multi-agency child protection; proactive and assertive approach
- [[multi-agency-working]] — information sharing across health, education, and social services
- [[child-protection-failures]] — consequences of siloed services not communicating
