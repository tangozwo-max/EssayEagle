# M4 W1 P14 — Social development

This page traces social development from infancy through adolescence, emphasising the foundational role of attachment relationships in early social functioning. It describes how social skills progress from caregiver-dependent interactions in infancy to peer relationships and social role exploration in preschool, collaborative peer relationships in childhood, and identity-based friendships and romantic interests in adolescence.
