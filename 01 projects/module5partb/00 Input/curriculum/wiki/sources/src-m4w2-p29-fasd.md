# M4 W2 P29 — Fetal alcohol spectrum disorder

Detailed examination of FASD as a range of neurodevelopmental conditions resulting from prenatal alcohol exposure. Covers variable presentation based on timing, quantity/frequency of exposure, maternal factors, and fetal factors. Describes diagnostic facial features and wide-ranging cognitive, motor, and behavioral impacts. Notes FASD as example of teratogenic exposure.
