# Long-Term Potentiation (LTP)

Long-lasting **enhancement** in synaptic strength following repeated stimulation of a synapse. Specific type of long-term synaptic plasticity. Crucial for learning and memory.

People with good memories often have more efficient LTP and increased AMPA receptor trafficking at the synapse.
