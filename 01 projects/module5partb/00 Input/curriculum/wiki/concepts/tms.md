# Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS)

Non-invasive electromagnetic stimulation of neurons ~2 cm below the scalp via a magnetic coil delivering repetitive magnetic pulses. Induces long-lasting plasticity.

## Approved uses
Depression, obsessive-compulsive disorder, addiction.
