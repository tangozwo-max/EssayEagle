# Article 3 — European Convention on Human Rights

> "No one shall be subjected to torture or to inhuman and degrading treatment or punishment."

Mental-health professionals who restrain or seclude may violate Article 3, even where the aim is therapeutic or safety-driven.

## Key case — Aerts vs Belgium
A person detained in a prison psychiatric wing claimed Article 3 breach. European Court of Human Rights found **no violation**, judging living conditions had not conclusively caused inhuman/degrading treatment effects on mental health.
