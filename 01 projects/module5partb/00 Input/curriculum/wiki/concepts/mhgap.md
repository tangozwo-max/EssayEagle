# mhGAP — WHO Mental Health Gap Action Programme

WHO program addressing the global **treatment gap** (majority of people with MH disorders receive no treatment, especially in LMICs).

## 5 Keys to Effective Interventions
1. **Psychoeducation** — teach about the condition
2. **Reduce stress and strengthen support** — stress management, social support
3. **Promote functioning in daily activities** — behavioral activation, MI
4. **Brief psychosocial treatment** — PST, CBT, IPT
5. **Pharmacology** — antidepressants when indicated

## mhGAP-HIG
Humanitarian Intervention Guide: for populations affected by humanitarian crises. Strategies: psychoeducation, stress management, social support strengthening.
