# Cytokines

Small proteins released by immune-system cells; coordinate response to infection/injury. Chronic inflammation → overproduction of **pro-inflammatory cytokines** → mood imbalance, depression, stress.

## Key inflammation markers in depression
- **CRP** — C-reactive protein
- **IL-6** — Interleukin-6
- **TNF-α** — Tumour necrosis factor-α
