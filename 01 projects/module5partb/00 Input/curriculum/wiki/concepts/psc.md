# Pediatric Symptom Checklist (PSC)

The PSC is a screening instrument designed to identify and assess psychosocial issues that may affect a child's functioning. It is administered to children and young people aged 4–16 years.

## Key characteristics

- **Age range**: 4–16 years
- **Purpose**: identifies and assesses psychosocial issues affecting child functioning
- **Multiple completion methods**: parent, teacher, or self-report questionnaire
- **Standardised**: validated screening tool with established cut-off scores

## Administration

The PSC can be completed by:
- Parents
- Teachers
- Mental health workers
- Children and young people themselves

## Clinical use

When screening indicates psychosocial difficulties, further assessment and intervention are recommended.
