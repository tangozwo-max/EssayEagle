# Hypervigilance and Emotional Numbness

Two contrasting PTSD presentations:

- **Hypervigilance** — amygdala-driven; chronic heightened arousal, scanning for threat.
- **Emotional numbness / detachment** — paradoxical. May be driven by *increased* medial PFC activity dampening amygdala and emotional-expression regions (opposite mechanism).
