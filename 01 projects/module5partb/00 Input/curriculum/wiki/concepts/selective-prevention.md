# Selective Prevention

Interventions targeting **subgroups at elevated risk** of developing a disorder — based on biological, psychological, or social risk factors — but who are not yet symptomatic.

Examples: stress-management programmes for first responders, resilience training for children in conflict zones, parenting support for low-income families.
