# Pathway to Disease — Discrimination as Chronic Psychosocial Stress

Chronic stress from stigma and discrimination becomes biological via the neuroendocrine system.

```
Psychosocial stress (stigma, discrimination)
         │
         ▼
HPA-axis activation → Cortisol ↑    +    Stress-NT systems (DA, NA, 5-HT, NT)
         │                                    │
         └──────────────┬─────────────────────┘
                        ▼
            Functional brain networks affected:
            Salience Network · Prefrontal Cortex
                        │
                        ▼
            Metabolic · Immune · Mood · Cognitive changes
```

## Empirical foundation
Ethnic-minority populations experiencing discrimination show higher rates of psychosis, schizophrenia, and mood disorders — associated with discrimination itself, **not** ethnicity.
