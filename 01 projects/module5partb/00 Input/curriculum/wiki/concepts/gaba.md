# GABA — Gamma-aminobutyric acid

Primary **inhibitory** neurotransmitter. Balances glutamate; essential for regulating muscle tone, controlling anxiety, contributing to relaxation. Low GABA implicated in schizophrenia, autism spectrum disorder (ASD), and ADHD.
