# Beta-Blockers

A class of drugs that block **β-adrenergic receptors**, reducing the physical symptoms of anxiety and stress (tachycardia, tremor, sweating) without sedating the CNS.

Examples: **propranolol**, atenolol, metoprolol.

## Mental health uses
- **Performance anxiety / situational anxiety**: propranolol 10–40 mg PRN before trigger (exams, public speaking)
- Adjunct in PTSD (propranolol may reduce reconsolidation of fear memories — still investigational)
- Management of akathisia (restlessness from antipsychotics)

Not appropriate for chronic generalised anxiety (no effect on cognitive/emotional components).
