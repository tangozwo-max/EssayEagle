# Neural Stem Cells, NPCs, Neuroblasts

Cell lineage for adult neurogenesis.

- **Neural stem cells** — programmed to become neurons or glial cells; divide to proliferate.
- **Neural progenitor cells (NPCs)** — arise from stem cells; can become neurons, astrocytes, or oligodendrocytes.
- **Neuroblasts** — intermediate stage. Migrate to hippocampus; extend axons/dendrites; form initial synaptic framework. Mature into functional neurons.
