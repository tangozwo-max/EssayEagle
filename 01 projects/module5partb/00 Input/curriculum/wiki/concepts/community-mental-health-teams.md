# Community Mental Health Teams (CMHT)

Multidisciplinary teams in secondary (community) care. Support people with complex MH needs within a specific age range.

Typical composition: nurses, OTs, social workers, psychologists, psychiatrists, support workers / HCAs.

Specialist variants include perinatal-MH teams and early-intervention-in-psychosis (EIP) teams.
