# Sympathetic vs Parasympathetic Nervous System

Two branches of the [[autonomic-nervous-system]].

## Sympathetic NS (accelerator)
Prepares body for fight-or-flight:
- ↑ Heart rate
- Pupils dilate
- Respiration quickens
- Digestion inhibited
- Blood flow redirected to muscles

## Parasympathetic NS (brake)
Promotes rest-and-digest:
- ↓ Heart rate
- Pupils constrict
- Breathing slows
- Digestion stimulated
- Energy storage promoted
