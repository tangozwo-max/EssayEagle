# Ketamine

Non-competitive NMDA-receptor antagonist. Originally an anaesthetic (approved 1970, Vietnam battlefield use). Rapid antidepressant effect discovered 1990s — symptoms improve within **hours**, lasting weeks (vs SSRI 4-week wait).

## Proposed mechanisms
- NMDA antagonism (channel blocker + allosteric binding)
- Anti-inflammatory effects
- Raises [[bdnf]] levels → neuroplasticity
- AMPA stimulation → new synapses

## Clinical use
- [[esketamine]] (Spravato) FDA-approved 2019 as nasal spray for TRD.
- Only approved for moderate-severe depression after ≥2 failed antidepressants.
- Risks: addiction, dependency, off-label misuse; street use causes overdose fatalities.
