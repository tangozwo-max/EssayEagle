# Primordial Prevention

The earliest level of prevention — **preventing the emergence of risk factors before they develop** in a population. Targets social, economic, and environmental conditions that give rise to health risks.

Examples: urban planning for green spaces, poverty reduction programmes, national nutrition policies.

Operates upstream of [[universal-prevention]] in the [[prevention-spectrum]].
