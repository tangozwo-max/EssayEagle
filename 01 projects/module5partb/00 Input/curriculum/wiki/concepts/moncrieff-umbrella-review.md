# Moncrieff Umbrella Review — The Serotonin Theory of Depression (2023)

Systematic umbrella review concluding: **"no convincing evidence that depression is associated with, or caused by, lower serotonin concentrations or activity."** Covered six main areas: serotonin and 5-HIAA studies, receptor studies, transporter studies, depletion studies, genetics, and modelling.

Considerable debate followed in BMJ and other journals.
