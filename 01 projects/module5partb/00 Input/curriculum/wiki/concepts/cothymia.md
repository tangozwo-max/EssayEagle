# Cothymia

Simultaneous diagnoses of both **anxiety AND depression at case level**. ~50% of primary care patients with MH conditions have both.

## Prognosis
A 30-year follow-up study in Nottingham found cothymia had **significantly worse prognosis** than either panic disorder or GAD alone.

## Neurochemical basis
Both anxiety and depression involve disturbances in **serotonin and norepinephrine** transmission — suggesting shared neurobiological mechanisms.

## Diagnostic approaches
1. **Separate diagnoses**: DSM-5 and ICD-11 maintain separate categories
2. **Mixed anxiety-depressive disorder** (ICD-11): neither sufficiently severe for individual diagnosis
3. **Anxious depression** (proposed): both at case level ≥2 weeks
