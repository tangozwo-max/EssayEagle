# SPECT — Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography

Gamma-emitting tracer attached to a biological molecule; a rotating gamma camera detects the tracer. Measures **blood flow only** (not metabolism). 3D image reconstructed via computer.

## Uses
- Head trauma
- Brain tumours
- Cerebrovascular disorders
- Parkinson's disease
- Atypical dementia cases
- Differentiating Lewy Body dementia from Parkinsonism
- Epilepsy
