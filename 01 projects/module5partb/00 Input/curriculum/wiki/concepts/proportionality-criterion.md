# Proportionality Criterion

Ethical test for [[coercive-measures]]: assesses the **probability and impact of potential harm** the measure aims to prevent vs. **the harm caused by the measure itself**. A coercive measure is justified **only** if prevented harm is proportional to inflicted harm. (Sashidharan et al.)
