# Three Tests of Ethical Decision-Making

Quick check applied in step 6 of [[apna-seven-steps]].

| Test | Question |
|---|---|
| **Justice** | Would you treat other people the same in similar situations? |
| **Publicity** | Would you be comfortable with your course of action reported in the media? |
| **Universality** | Would you recommend the same action to another practitioner? |

If any test fails, **return to the start of the decision process**.
