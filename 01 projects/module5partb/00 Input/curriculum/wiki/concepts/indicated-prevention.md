# Indicated Prevention

Interventions targeting **individuals who already show early signs or symptoms** of a disorder but do not yet meet full diagnostic criteria.

Examples: Brief counselling for adolescents with subclinical depression, CBT-based early intervention programmes for first-episode psychosis risk.

Highest-risk tier of the universal–selective–indicated hierarchy. Overlaps conceptually with [[stepped-care-model]] entry points.
