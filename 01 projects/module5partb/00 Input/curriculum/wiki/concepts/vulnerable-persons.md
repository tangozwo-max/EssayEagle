# Vulnerable Persons

People who require **special ethical attention** due to reduced capacity, power, or protection. Central to the Justice principle ([[four-principles-bioethics]]).

## Categories named in the curriculum
- Individuals with mental-health conditions
- People who have experienced traumatic events
- Children, elderly, residents of nursing homes / hospices / institutions
- People with serious underlying health conditions
- Prisoners
- Pregnant women
- Individuals who do not speak the first language of the practitioner
