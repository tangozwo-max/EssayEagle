# Postnatal Depression

Depressive episode occurring in the postpartum period (usually within 4 weeks, can extend to 1 year).

## Risk factors
- Previous MH history (especially depression)
- MH problems during pregnancy
- Lack of social support / difficult relationship
- Stressful life events or trauma
- Domestic violence
- First-time mother (radical role change)

## Biological factors
Dramatic drop in **estrogen and progesterone** after birth. Thyroid hormones also drop — can cause fatigue, sluggishness, depression.

## Distinction
- **Baby blues**: brief (3-5 days), resolves spontaneously
- **Postnatal depression**: persists >2 weeks, significant impairment
- **Postpartum psychosis**: severe, rare — hallucinations, delusions
