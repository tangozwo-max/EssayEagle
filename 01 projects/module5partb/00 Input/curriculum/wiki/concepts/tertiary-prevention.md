# Tertiary Prevention

Reducing the **impact and complications of established disease** — focused on rehabilitation, reducing disability, and improving quality of life.

For mental health: relapse prevention programmes, supported employment for people with schizophrenia, peer support in recovery communities.

Part of the primordial–primary–secondary–tertiary–quaternary hierarchy. Distinguished from [[quaternary-prevention]] (avoiding iatrogenic harm from over-treatment).
