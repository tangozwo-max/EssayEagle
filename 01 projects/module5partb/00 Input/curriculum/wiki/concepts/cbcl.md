# Child Behavior Checklist (CBCL)

The CBCL is a parent-report questionnaire designed to assess a range of behavioural and emotional problems in children and young people aged 6–18 years.

## Key characteristics

- **Age range**: 6–18 years
- **Format**: parent-report questionnaire
- **Scope**: assesses behavioural and emotional difficulties
- **Standardised**: validated instrument with established norms
- **Comprehensive**: measures multiple domains of child behaviour and emotion

## Administration

The CBCL is typically completed by parents or primary caregivers who report on their child's behaviour and emotional functioning.

## Clinical use

Scores above clinical cut-off thresholds indicate need for further assessment and potential intervention for behavioural or emotional difficulties.

## Strengths

- Well-established and widely used
- Parent/caregiver perspective
- Comprehensive assessment
