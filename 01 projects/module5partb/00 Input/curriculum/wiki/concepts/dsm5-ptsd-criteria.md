# DSM-5 PTSD Criteria

Under **Trauma- and Stressor-Related Disorders** in DSM-5. Based on self-reported symptoms (no biomarker test exists).

## The five criteria
- **A**: Exposure to trauma
- **B**: Intrusive thoughts
- **C**: Avoidance of trauma stimuli
- **D**: Negative alterations in mood and cognition
- **E**: Alterations in arousal and reactivity

## Additional requirements
- Symptoms persist > 1 month
- Cause distress or functional impairment
- Not attributable to medication, substance use, or other illness
