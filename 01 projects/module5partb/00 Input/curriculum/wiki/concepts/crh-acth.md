# CRH and ACTH

Two hormones in the [[hpa-axis]] that transmit the stress signal from brain to body.

## CRH — Corticotropin-Releasing Hormone
Secreted by the **hypothalamus** in response to stress signals. Acts on the pituitary gland to trigger ACTH release.

## ACTH — Adrenocorticotropic Hormone
Released by the **anterior pituitary gland** on CRH signalling. Travels via the bloodstream to the **adrenal cortex**, stimulating synthesis and release of [[cortisol]].

## Flow
Stressor → Hypothalamus (CRH) → Anterior pituitary (ACTH) → Adrenal cortex ([[cortisol]])
