# Premenstrual Dysphoric Disorder (PMDD)

Severe form of premenstrual syndrome with mood and depressive symptoms linked to the menstrual cycle's luteal phase. Associated with HPA axis dysregulation; often lower basal cortisol in follicular and luteal phases with blunted stress response.
