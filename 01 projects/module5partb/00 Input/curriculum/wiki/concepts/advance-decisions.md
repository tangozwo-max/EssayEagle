# Advance Decisions

Instructions about medical and healthcare treatments a person wishes to **refuse in the future** if they lose capacity. Drawn up while the person has capacity (18+).

- **Legally binding** — health professionals must follow them.
- Exception: emergency where it's unclear whether the advance decision covers the treatment.
- Can be oral or written.
