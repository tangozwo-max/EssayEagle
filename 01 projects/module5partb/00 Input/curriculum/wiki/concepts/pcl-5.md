# PCL-5 — PTSD Checklist for DSM-5

Validated **20-item self-report questionnaire** for assessing presence and severity of PTSD symptoms. [Weathers et al.]

## Uses
- Quantify/monitor PTSD symptoms over time
- Screen for PTSD
- Provisional diagnosis

## Interpretation
- **Total score**: 0–80; cutoff **31–33** = probable PTSD
- **DSM-5 diagnostic rule**: items rated ≥2 count as endorsed
  - ≥1 Criterion B item (questions 1–5: re-experiencing)
  - ≥1 Criterion C item (questions 6–7: avoidance)
  - ≥2 Criterion D items (questions 8–14: negative cognitions)
  - ≥2 Criterion E items (questions 15–20: arousal/reactivity)

## Caution
NOT a standalone diagnostic tool. Supplement with clinical interview and structured assessment.
