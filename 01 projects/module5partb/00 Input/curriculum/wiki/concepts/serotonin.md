# Serotonin (5-HT)

Regulates mood, sleep, appetite, digestion. Contributes to feelings of well-being and happiness. Imbalances associated with depression, anxiety, mood disorders. Most common target of [[ssri]] antidepressants.

~90 % of body's serotonin is produced in the gut — key player in the [[gut-brain-axis]].
