# Positivism

Philosophical system associated with **Auguste Comte**. Asserts that knowledge must be grounded in **observable phenomena, measurable data, and verifiable facts**. Rejects metaphysical or speculative explanations. Emerged during the Scientific Revolution.

## Impact on medicine
- Promoted evidence-based practices and standardization
- Aligned with the [[biomedical-model-medicine]] — disease as deviation from measurable biological norm
- Reduced focus on psychosocial and spiritual dimensions of health
