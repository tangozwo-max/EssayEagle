# Medical Model of Mental Health

Treats mental-health conditions **the same way as physical illnesses** — assuming an organic or physical cause. Focuses on diagnosing and treating the condition medically, with biological factors (genetics, neurotransmitters, neurophysiology, neuroanatomy) at the core.

Dominant in mid-20th century. Inpatient MH services, community MH services, and services providing both mental and physical health care (e.g., dementia) are often led by medically trained psychiatrists within this tradition.

## Contrast
- [[biopsychosocial-model]] — broader, multifactorial alternative.
- Personal-recovery / strength-based approaches.
