# Serotonin Hypothesis of Depression

Extension of [[monoamine-deficiency-hypothesis]]: depression caused by low serotonin levels. Basis for SSRI development in the 1970s.

## Problems
- ~4-week delay in clinical effect vs near-immediate NT increase.
- Moncrieff et al. umbrella review (2023): "no convincing evidence that depression is associated with, or caused by, lower serotonin concentrations or activity."
- ~90 % of public still believes the hypothesis.
