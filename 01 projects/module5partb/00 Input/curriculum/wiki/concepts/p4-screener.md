# P4 Screener

Validated tool for assessing suicide risk. Used in primary care and oncology settings.

## Four domains
1. **Past attempts** — Has the person made any past suicide attempts?
2. **Plan** — Does the person have a suicide plan?
3. **Probability** — How probable is it they will act on the plan?
4. **Preventive factors** — What is stopping them?

## Classification
- **Minimal risk**: few positive answers
- **Lower risk**: some positive
- **Higher risk**: multiple positive answers, especially high probability

## Implementation
- Begin with: "Have you thought about harming yourself?"
- If YES → proceed to P4 questions
- Keep conversational, NOT mechanical
- More bold answers = higher risk
