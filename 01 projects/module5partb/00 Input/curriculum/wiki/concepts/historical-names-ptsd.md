# Historical Names for PTSD

| War | Name |
|---|---|
| US Civil War | **Soldier's heart** |
| WWI | **Shell shock** |
| WWII | **Combat fatigue** |
| Vietnam War | **Delayed stress** |

Condition "forgotten" between wars. PTSD recognised as formal diagnosis only in **1980** — largely due to Vietnam veterans and advocates. George W. Bush advocated dropping the "D" (disorder) to address stigma — "Post-traumatic stress is an injury; it's not a disorder."
