# Classical Conditioning (Ivan Pavlov, 1849–1936)

Russian physiologist; demonstrated that a neutral stimulus (bell) paired repeatedly with an unconditioned stimulus (food) comes to elicit a conditioned response (salivation).

**Unconditioned stimulus + Neutral stimulus → Conditioned stimulus → Conditioned response**

## Clinical relevance
- Basis for phobia formation
- Underlies exposure therapy (extinction of conditioned fear)
- Foundation of behaviourism
