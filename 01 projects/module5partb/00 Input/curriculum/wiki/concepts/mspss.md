# Multidimensional Scale of Perceived Social Support (MSPSS)

12-item psychometric tool (Zimet et al.) measuring perceived social support across three sources: **family, friends, significant others** (4 items each). 7-point Likert scale. Subscales or total score.

Low MSPSS scores → increased risk of depression, anxiety, PTSD.
