# Electroconvulsive Therapy (ECT)

Medical treatment primarily for **severe, treatment-resistant (intractable) depression** and other severe mental-health conditions. Passes electrical currents through the brain to trigger a brief seizure under **general anaesthesia** in controlled conditions.

## Mechanism
- Alters neurotransmitter and neurohormonal levels.
- Stimulates nerve-cell growth.
- Normalises connectivity between brain regions.
- Promotes [[neuroplasticity]].
- Exact mechanism not fully understood.

## Ethical issues
- **Informed consent** — critics argue patients don't fully understand risks/benefits.
- **Stigma and misconceptions** — historical misrepresentation in media leads to fear, reluctance.
