# Six Core Ethics Skills (Weiss Roberts & Termuehlen)

Set of skills mental health professionals rely on in everyday practice.

1. **Identify ethical issues as they arise** — intuition or logical analysis.
2. **Understand your own values, beliefs, and sense of self** — guards against [[implicit-bias]].
3. **Be aware of the limits of your own knowledge and experience** — practice within competence.
4. **Recognise high-risk situations** — cases where harm prevention may override usual autonomy.
5. **Seek information and consultation** — use guidance from colleagues and literature.
6. **Build appropriate ethical safeguards into your work** — structured reflective practice, supervision.
