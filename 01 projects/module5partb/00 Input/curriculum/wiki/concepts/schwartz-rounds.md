# Schwartz Rounds

Structured, interdisciplinary reflective-practice meetings where staff listen and share in a supportive environment. Topic examples: "a patient I will never forget." Aim: create a culture of **compassion and belonging**.

## Goals
- Reflect on holistic needs of those under care
- Discuss how best to meet needs and preferences
- Reflect on actions and ethical fit
- Improve team's decision-making confidence
- Identify professional/personal biases ([[implicit-bias]])
- Identify self-care needs for staff
