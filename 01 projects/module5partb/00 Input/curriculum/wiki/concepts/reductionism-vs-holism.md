# Reductionism vs Holism

- **Reductionism** — complex phenomena (e.g., depression) explained by simpler elements (e.g., serotonin levels).
- **Holism** — accounts for multifactorial determinants across biological, psychological, social, environmental factors.

## Antireductionist critique (Moncrieff et al. 2024)
Causation in depression involves meaning and interpretation, not mechanistic causation. Depression may be a normal brain **responding to adversity**, not a dysfunctional one.
