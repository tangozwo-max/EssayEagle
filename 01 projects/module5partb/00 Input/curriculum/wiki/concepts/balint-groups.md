# Balint Groups

Group-discussion format for healthcare professionals introduced by **Dr. Michael Balint** (Hungarian psychoanalyst) in the 1950s. Uses case presentations to explore the **emotional and psychological aspects** of clinician-patient relationships.

## Format
- Regular small-group meetings
- Facilitator encourages safe, confidential sharing
- Focus on relationship dynamics, emotional responses, ethical concerns, interpersonal dynamics

## Benefits
- Insight into own behaviour, communication patterns, emotional reactions
- Reduces burnout
- Complements [[schwartz-rounds]]
