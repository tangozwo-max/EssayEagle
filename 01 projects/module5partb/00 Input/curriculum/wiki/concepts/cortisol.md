# Cortisol

The primary glucocorticoid hormone released by the **adrenal glands** in response to [[acth]] signalling from the pituitary. Commonly called the **"stress hormone."**

## Function
- Mobilises energy (raises blood glucose).
- Suppresses non-essential systems (immune, digestion, reproduction) during acute threat.
- Modulates mood, memory, and fear learning.

## Dysregulation
- **Chronic elevation** → depression, anxiety, HPA-axis dysfunction.
- Persistent developmental exposure → altered reward, memory, threat networks ([[hpa-axis]]).
- Affects the [[salience-network]] leading to sustained hypervigilance.
