# EMDR — Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing

Therapy involving **bilateral stimulation** (eye movements, alternating tactile/auditory stimuli) while the person recalls traumatic memories. Believed to help the brain process trauma memories with reduced emotional distress.

## Clinical position (UK)
Consider for ages 7–17 with PTSD or significant PTSD symptoms > 3 months post-trauma, **only if** they don't respond to or engage with trauma-focused CBT.
