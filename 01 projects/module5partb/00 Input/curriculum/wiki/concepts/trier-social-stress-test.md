# Trier Social Stress Test (TSST)

Standardised research procedure (early 1990s) to reliably induce psychological stress and measure physiological/psychological responses.

## Protocol
1. **Preparation** — 5–10 min to prepare a speech (usually about job suitability).
2. **Speech task** — deliver speech before a neutral/negative evaluator panel (no feedback).
3. **Mental arithmetic** — challenging out-loud serial subtraction; restart from beginning if error.

Induces **2–3× cortisol spike** in ~70–80 % of participants. Saliva/blood/urine samples collected before, during, after.
