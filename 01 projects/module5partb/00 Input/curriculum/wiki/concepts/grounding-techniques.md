# Grounding Techniques

Methods to return an overwhelmed person to the present environment (the "daylight"). Critical during trauma-screening if a client dissociates or has intense emotional response.

## Examples
- Name red objects in the room (visual grounding)
- 5-4-3-2-1 (5 see, 4 touch, 3 hear, 2 smell, 1 taste)
- Focus on breath or a physical anchor
