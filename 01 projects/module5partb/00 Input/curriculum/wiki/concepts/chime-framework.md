# CHIME Framework

Person-centred, holistic framework for mental-health recovery. Originated in the personal-recovery literature (Leamy et al.).

| Letter | Element | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **C** | Connectedness | Peer support, social groups, relationships, community |
| **H** | Hope and optimism | Belief in recovery, hope-inspiring relationships, aspirations |
| **I** | Identity | Rebuilding positive sense of self, overcoming stigma |
| **M** | Meaning | Meaning in MH experiences, meaningful life and social roles |
| **E** | Empowerment | Personal responsibility, control, focus on strengths |
