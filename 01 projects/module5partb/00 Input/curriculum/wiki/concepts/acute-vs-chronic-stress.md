# Acute vs Chronic Stress

- **Acute stress** — one-off immediate event (snake attack, hand burn). Activates [[sam-axis]]. Body returns to homeostasis once threat resolves.
- **Chronic stress** — prolonged, persistent psychological stress (work, relationships, poverty). Activates [[hpa-axis]]. Can last days, months, years. Estimated to underlie **~80 %** of modern illnesses.

Early-life chronic stress → permanent epigenetic, endocrine, neural, immune, and inflammatory changes; risk of MDD boosted 59–75 %; anxiety disorders 1.9–3.6× more common.
