# Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)

Excessive, uncontrollable worry about multiple events/activities more days than not for ≥6 months. Somatic symptoms (restlessness, fatigue, concentration difficulty, muscle tension, sleep disturbance).

Screening: [[gad-7]] (mild 5, moderate 10, severe 15).
