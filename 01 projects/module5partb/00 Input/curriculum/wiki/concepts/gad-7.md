# GAD-7

7-item self-report questionnaire developed by **Spitzer et al.** to screen for GAD. Also useful for panic disorder, SAD, and PTSD.

## Items (past 2 weeks frequency)
Feeling nervous/anxious, inability to stop worrying, worrying about different things, trouble relaxing, restlessness, irritability, feeling something awful might happen.

## Scoring
| Score | Severity |
|---|---|
| 0–4 | No anxiety |
| 5–9 | Mild |
| 10–14 | Moderate |
| 15–21 | Severe |

Scale: 0 (not at all) → 3 (nearly every day). Max = 21.

## Limitations
- Self-report: cultural variations in anxiety expression
- Does not account for physical comorbidities that mimic anxiety
