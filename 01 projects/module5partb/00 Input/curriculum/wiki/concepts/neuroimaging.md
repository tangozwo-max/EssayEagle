# Neuroimaging Overview

Medical-imaging technologies providing insights into brain structure and function. Began with 1870s detection of brain electrical activity.

## Main categories
- **Structural** — [[smri]], [[ct-scan]]
- **Functional** — [[fmri]] (BOLD response), [[pet-scan]], [[spect]], [[eeg]], [[meg]]
- **Connectivity** — [[dmri-dti]] (white-matter tracts)

## History milestones
- 1924: Hans Berger — first EEG device for humans
- 1960s: David Cohen — MEG signals; later the SQUID sensor
- 1972: Raymond Damadian — first MRI patent
- 1976: First human PET image
- 1977: First human sMRI
- 1990s: Ogawa/Kwong — fMRI (BOLD)
