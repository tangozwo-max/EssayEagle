# CNS and PNS

- **CNS (Central Nervous System)** — brain and spinal cord.
- **PNS (Peripheral Nervous System)** — nerves branching from the spinal cord throughout the body, communicating with the CNS.

The [[limbic-system]] is a subset of the CNS with specialised emotional, motivational, and memory functions.
