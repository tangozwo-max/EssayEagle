# Care Programme Approach (CPA)

UK framework for care planning used by secondary-care mental-health teams. Includes:
- Clinical history (mood, triggers, family, etc.)
- Social and personal functioning
- Psychosocial stressors
- Comorbidities, differential diagnoses
- Physical health
- Service-use history
- **Recovery goals**
- Risk-management plan (self-neglect, self-harm, finance, relationships)

Shared with the person's family and primary-care doctor.
