# Stepped-Care Model

UK NHS Talking Therapies framework. Allocates people to increasing intensity of treatment based on current condition severity. If condition doesn't improve → step up; if improves → step down.

## Typical structure
Step 1: Watchful waiting / self-help materials
Step 2: Guided self-help, low-intensity psychological interventions
Step 3: High-intensity psychological therapy (CBT), medication
Step 4: Specialist mental health services
Step 5: Inpatient care, crisis services

Delivery: in person, by phone, group workshops, online platforms.
