# Qualitative vs Quantitative Research

## Qualitative
Explores subjective experiences, perceptions, and meanings. Richness of human experience over numerical data.

Main approaches:
- **Narrative** — stories as data
- **Phenomenology** — lived experience of a phenomenon
- **Grounded theory** — theory emerging from data
- **Ethnography** — immersion in cultural setting
- **Case study** — deep analysis of individual/group/event

## Quantitative
Numerical data → test hypotheses, establish causality using statistical tools.

**Advantages:** fast data collection, validated tools, reliable large-sample evidence, remote/tech-driven collection possible, anonymisation.
**Disadvantages:** requires large samples, expensive, statistical-software literacy required, tool-development slow, limited by variables and bias.

## Mixed-methods
Combines both — statistical outcomes + participant experience.
