# Major Depressive Disorder (MDD)

Serious mood disorder. Antidepressants fail in ~**30 %** of people, indicating neurobiology is more complex than a simple NT imbalance.

## Treatment principles
- Balance of approaches: medication (SSRIs, ketamine), therapy (CBT), lifestyle.
- Remission is the formal treatment goal; associated with restoration of functioning and well-being.
