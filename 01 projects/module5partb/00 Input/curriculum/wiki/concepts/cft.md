# Compassion-Focused Therapy (CFT)

**Third-wave therapy** using a Buddhist-influenced definition of compassion. Developed by Paul Gilbert.

## Target
People high in **shame and self-criticism** — common in trauma survivors, CPTSD, and personality disorders.

## Goals
Generate:
- **Self-compassion** — being kind to oneself in suffering
- **Compassion to others**
- **Openness to compassion from others**

## Skills developed
Compassionate reasoning, behavior, imagery, feeling, sensation

## Evidence
Systematic review supports psychotherapeutic benefits. Recommended for Sally (M2 W6) to process childhood trauma and self-blame.
