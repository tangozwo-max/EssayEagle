# Prevention Intervention Levels — Universal / Selective / Indicated

Three audience-based categories for preventive interventions (extending the [[prevention-levels-who]] primary/secondary/tertiary scheme).

| Level | Target | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Universal** | Whole population / general public | Mental-health component in school curriculum |
| **Selective** | High-risk groups showing no symptoms | Program for children with high [[ace-score]] |
| **Indicated** | Individuals with sub-clinical symptoms, not yet meeting diagnostic criteria | Early intervention for someone struggling with substance use |
| **Treatment** | Diagnosed individuals | Targeted clinical therapy |
