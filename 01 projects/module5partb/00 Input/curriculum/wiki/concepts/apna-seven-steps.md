# APNA Seven-Step Ethical Decision-Making

Systematic framework from the **American Psychiatric Nurses Association (APNA)** for addressing ethical dilemmas.

1. **Identify the problem** — gather facts, distinguish ethical from clinical/legal/personal issue.
2. **Apply the appropriate code of ethics** — respect dignity, autonomy, boundaries.
3. **Determine the scope of the dilemma** — which principles apply, which take precedence.
4. **Think about next steps** — identify roles needed for ongoing care.
5. **Consider potential consequences** — pros/cons, ripple effects for family/employers.
6. **Evaluate the course of action** — apply the [[three-tests-ethical-decision-making]].
7. **Implement the course of action** — seek peer and organisational support.
