# Intersectionality

Studies the meaning and consequences of belonging to **multiple stigmatised social groups** at once (e.g., Black + MH condition + low socioeconomic status).

## Two core aspects (Oexle et al.)
- **Prominence** — how important a difference is to others in a given context
- **Double (multiple) disadvantage** — cumulative outcomes of public stigma across identities

People with multiple stigmatised characteristics face compounding disadvantage: fewer job offers, more biased care, more arrests, harder housing access.
