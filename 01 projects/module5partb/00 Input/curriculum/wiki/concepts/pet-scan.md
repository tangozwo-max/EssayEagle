# Positron Emission Tomography (PET)

Radioactive tracer (positron emitter) injected → positrons encounter electrons → emit photons → scanner detects. Measures **glucose metabolism and blood flow**. First human PET image: 1976.

## Uses in neuroimaging
- Brain-tumour grading, staging, treatment monitoring
- Differentiating Alzheimer's from other dementias
- Seizure localisation
- Hippocampal sclerosis diagnosis
