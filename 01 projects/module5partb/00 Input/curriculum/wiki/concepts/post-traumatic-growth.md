# Post-Traumatic Growth (PTG)

Positive psychological change experienced as a result of trauma or other highly challenging life events. Identified by **Richard Tedeschi & Lawrence Calhoun** in the 1990s.

Estimated **½ to 2/3 of trauma survivors** experience some form of growth.

## Manifestations
- New perspectives on life; shift in priorities
- Deeper relationships; greater compassion and empathy
- Increased personal strength and resilience
- Recognition of new possibilities
- Spiritual development

Coexists with but differs from resilience and PTSD.

## Key driver: **Social support**
- Group therapy, peer support
- Strength-based approaches
- Mindfulness and acceptance
- CBT and Hope Theory
