# Deprivation of Liberty Safeguards (DoLS)

Procedure in the UK MCA allowing lawful deprivation of liberty **only** in best interests and least restrictive form.

- Applied in care homes and hospitals.
- At home / supported accommodation / shared placements → requires Court of Protection permission.
