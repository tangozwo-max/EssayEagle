# Neuroplasticity

Brain's ability to **change and adapt** in response to experience, learning, and injury. Encompasses synaptic plasticity, structural plasticity, and adult neurogenesis.

## Key drivers
- Physical exercise, diet
- Cognitive training
- Stress-reduction (mindfulness, meditation, yoga)
- CBT
- ECT, TMS, MST
- Ketamine, SSRIs, lithium, intermittent fasting
- BDNF signalling

> The common pathway of plasticity-driven changes for many therapies involves BDNF.
