# Broca's and Wernicke's Areas

Language-related brain regions identified in the 1800s.

- **Broca's area** — left frontal lobe; speech *production* (Pierre Broca, from 12 autopsies of patients who lost speech but kept comprehension).
- **Wernicke's area** — posterior left temporal lobe; language *comprehension* (Karl Wernicke).
