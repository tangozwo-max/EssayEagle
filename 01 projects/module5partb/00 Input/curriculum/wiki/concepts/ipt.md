# Interpersonal Psychotherapy (IPT)

Brief psychological therapy focused on improving **quality of interpersonal relationships and social functioning** to reduce distress.

## Addresses
- Interpersonal deficits and disputes
- Unresolved grief
- Life transitions
- Role disputes

## Distinction from other therapies
- Focus on **current relationships** and problems
- NOT on childhood or developmental history (unlike psychodynamic)
- More structured than non-directive counselling

## Evidence
Strong evidence for depression, perinatal depression. Used with Sally (comorbid CPTSD and relationship difficulties).
