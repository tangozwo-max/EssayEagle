# Stereotype Content Model (SCM)

People form stereotypes along **two axes**: warmth and competence. Four quadrants produce four characteristic emotional reactions.

| | Low competence | High competence |
|---|---|---|
| **High warmth** | **Pity** (elderly, disabled, children) | **Admiration** |
| **Low warmth** | **Contempt** | **Envy** (the rich, business people, technical experts) |

## Placement for people with MH conditions
- Generally stereotyped as **low warmth + low competence** → contempt.
- People with mood disorders — moderate range.
- People with schizophrenia — low on both.
