# Ayurveda

Ancient Indian medical tradition. Key features:

- **Mind = a sense organ AND a motor organ** — material in nature (composed of five elements: fire, air, earth, water, space)
- Controls other five sense organs
- Mind, body, and consciousness are three elements of a whole person — each can influence the others
- Disease = imbalance between body, mind, and consciousness
- **Attitudes are elements of cure**

## Contrast with Cartesian dualism
Unlike Descartes, Ayurveda does not separate mind from body. Aligned with the phenomenological concept of the [[phenomenology-lived-body]].
