# Distributive Justice

A sub-principle of **Justice** ([[four-principles-bioethics]]): the fair, equitable, and appropriate distribution of healthcare resources — allocated so that all individuals have **equal opportunity to access services**.

## Practical issues
- Global inequity between [[hic-lmic]].
- Advanced neurotechnology rarely available in LMICs — compromises justice even when beneficence and nonmaleficence are upheld.
- Reflected in WHO CSDH [[csdh-framework]] action principle #2: "tackle the inequitable distribution of power and resources."
