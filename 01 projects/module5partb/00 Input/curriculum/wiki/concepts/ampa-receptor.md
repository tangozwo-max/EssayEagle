# AMPA Receptor

α-amino-3-hydroxy-5-methyl-4-isoxazole propionic acid receptor. Glutamate receptor enabling **rapid excitatory signalling**. When glutamate binds → gate opens → Na⁺ enters → neuron fires. Important for everyday movement, thinking, environmental response.

Ketamine's antidepressant effect may involve AMPA stimulation → new synapse generation.
