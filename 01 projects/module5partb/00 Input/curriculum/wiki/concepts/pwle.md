# People With Lived Experience (PWLE)

People who have personal experience of mental-health conditions. Experts by experience — increasingly recognised as leaders and change agents.

## Contributions
- Expertise
- In-depth knowledge of navigating systems
- Experience of segregation/discrimination
- Cost-effective, innovative, practical solutions

## Emerging roles
- Support group facilitators
- Peer support specialists
- Social workers
- Psychiatrists with lived experience
- Lived-experience practitioners

## Barriers to inclusion
Social/cultural/environmental factors, negative language, persistent stereotypes, social determinants (unemployment, poverty, no access to education) that block leadership roles and service-development involvement. Fluctuating conditions make inflexible working hours challenging.
