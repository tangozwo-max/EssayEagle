# Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy (MBCT)

**Manualized, group-based skills training** program designed to prevent depressive relapse/recurrence. [Kuyken et al.]

## Core skills taught
- Awareness of **bodily sensations, thoughts, feelings** associated with depressive relapse
- Relate **constructively** to these experiences
- De-center from automatic negative thoughts

## Evidence
- As effective as **antidepressant medication** in preventing depressive relapse or recurrence
- Best evidence for patients with **3+ prior depressive episodes**
- Recommended for Isabella (M2 W6) if recurrent; primarily useful for recurrence prevention

## Format
8-week program, weekly 2-hour group sessions, daily home practice
