# BDI-II / BAI

- **BDI-II** — Beck Depression Inventory, 2nd edition. 21-item self-report scale for depression severity.
- **BAI** — Beck Anxiety Inventory. 21-item self-report scale for anxiety severity.

Used widely in research and clinical practice (Beck, Wright, Newman, Liese).
