# Promotion vs Prevention

Two complementary intervention philosophies in mental-health public health.

## Promotion
Interventions that **optimise positive mental health** by addressing determinants of positive mental health **before** any specific problem has been identified. Goal: improve population-level positive mental health.

## Prevention
Interventions that **minimise mental health problems** by addressing determinants of problems **before** a specific issue has been identified in the individual/group/population. Goal: reduce number of future problems.

## Scale of intensity (Prevention → Treatment)
From universal (everyone) → selective (at-risk groups) → indicated (sub-clinical symptoms) → treatment (diagnosed).
