# Dual-Process Model of Stigma

Challenges the idea that stigma is fully conscious/intentional. Stigma arises from **two modes of processing**:

- **Deliberate** — cognitive effort and control; can override initial impressions.
- **Automatic** — mental shortcuts (stereotypes) → fast reactions → [[implicit-bias]].

Emotion and behaviour can diverge: one may *feel* fear meeting someone with schizophrenia yet *act* with kindness.
