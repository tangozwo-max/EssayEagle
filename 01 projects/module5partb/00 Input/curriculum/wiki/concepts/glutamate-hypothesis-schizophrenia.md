# Glutamate Hypothesis of Schizophrenia

About **one-third** of people with schizophrenia don't respond to dopamine-targeting antipsychotics. Evidence (McCutcheon et al.) implicates **glutamate dysfunction** in these cases. Both dopamine and glutamate systems appear involved in schizophrenia pathophysiology.
