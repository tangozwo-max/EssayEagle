# Diffusion MRI / Diffusion Tensor Imaging (DTI)

Measures water diffusion along brain fibres — highlights **white-matter axon tracts** and their connectivity. 5–10 min scan; available on most MRI machines.

**DWI (Diffusion-Weighted Imaging)** is a related technique often used to grade lesions in acute stroke or multiple sclerosis.

- **Strengths**: white-matter mapping, sensitive to early tissue changes, non-invasive.
- **Limitations**: susceptible to movement/magnetic artifacts, complex processing, lower spatial resolution than sMRI.
