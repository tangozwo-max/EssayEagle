# Synaptic Pruning

Elimination of excess synapses during neurodevelopment to improve neural efficiency.

- Peaks ~age 2; continues through childhood (up to ~age 12).
- In **adolescence**, pruning focuses on the prefrontal cortex → improved decision-making and impulse control.
- Disruption linked to neurodevelopmental disorders.
