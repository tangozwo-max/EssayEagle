# NHS Long Term Plan (Mental Health element)

UK NHS strategy to **redesign community mental-health services** so they are:
- Place-based
- Integrated with primary-care networks
- Core community services aligned with PCNs, available at any level of need
- Wider specialist services organised to support the most complex needs while connecting to local MH services
