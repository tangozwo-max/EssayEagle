# YAM — Youth Aware of Mental Health

School-based **universal prevention program** for 13–17 year olds. 5 × 1-hour sessions over 3 weeks. Role-play and student-led discussions.

## Topics
Relationships with peers and adults, mood changes, sadness, stressful situations

## Evidence
EU-wide randomized controlled trial:
- ~50% reduction in new suicide attempts and severe suicidal ideation
- ~30% reduction in new depression cases
- Implemented with 85,000+ teenagers in 16 countries
