# Self-Stigma Progressive Model (4 phases)

Trickle-down process of stigma internalisation.

1. **Awareness** — socialisation makes the person aware of a stereotype.
2. **Personal agreement** — the individual agrees with the stereotype.
3. **Application** — the person with MH condition applies the stereotype to themselves.
4. **Harm** — shame, low self-esteem, depression; complicates recovery.

Most people experiencing MH conditions are at **earlier** phases — few progress to full internalisation.
