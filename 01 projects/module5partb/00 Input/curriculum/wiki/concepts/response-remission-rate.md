# Response vs Remission Rate

- **Response rate** — proportion of people showing *significant improvement* following an intervention (measured on standardised scales).
- **Remission rate** — proportion whose symptoms have decreased to the point they *no longer meet diagnostic criteria*.

**Remission** is the formal treatment goal for MDD, linked to restoration of functioning and well-being.
