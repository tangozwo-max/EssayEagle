# MADRS — Montgomery-Åsberg Depression Rating Scale

Clinician-rated 10-item scale for depressive severity: apparent sadness, reported sadness, inner tension, reduced sleep, reduced appetite, concentration difficulties, lassitude, inability to feel, pessimistic thoughts, suicidal thoughts.
