# Universal Prevention

Interventions targeting the **entire population** regardless of individual risk level. No screening required — benefits are expected to outweigh the low intensity of effort.

Examples: national mental health literacy campaigns, school-based social-emotional learning curricula, media guidelines for responsible reporting on suicide.

Contrasts with [[selective-prevention]] (targeted at risk groups) and [[indicated-prevention]] (targeted at symptomatic individuals).
