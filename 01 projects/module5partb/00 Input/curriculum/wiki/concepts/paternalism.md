# Paternalism

Situations where healthcare professionals **override the decisions and views** of people seeking care. Historically dominant in psychiatry; now considered acceptable **only** when a person demonstrably lacks decision-making capacity, and even then requires strong moral and legal justification.

## Shift away from paternalism
Modern mental healthcare has moved to **inclusive, rights-based models** (e.g., [[un-mental-health-principles]], [[who-qualityrights]]).
