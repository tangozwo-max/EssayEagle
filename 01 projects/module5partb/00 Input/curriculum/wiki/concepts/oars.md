# OARS

Core skills of **Motivational Interviewing**, essential for helping clients elicit motivation for change.

| Letter | Skill | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **O** | Open questions | Draw out and explore experiences, perspectives, ideas |
| **A** | Affirmations | Acknowledge strengths, efforts, past successes — build hope and confidence |
| **R** | Reflections | Careful listening; demonstrate understanding of what the person is saying |
| **S** | Summarising | Ensure shared understanding; reinforce key points from the service user |

## Additional MI skills
- **Language of change**: identifying "sustain talk" (against change) vs. "change talk" (for change)
- **Information exchange**: two-way street — both clinician and service user have expertise
