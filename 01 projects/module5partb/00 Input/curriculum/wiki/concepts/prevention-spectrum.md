# Prevention Spectrum

Two complementary classification systems for mental health prevention strategies.

## System 1: By timing/target
| Level | Target | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **Primordial** | Whole population | Structural/socioenvironmental factors before risk factors develop |
| **Primary** | Susceptible individuals | Limits exposure to risk |
| **Secondary** | Subclinical symptoms | Early detection and treatment |
| **Tertiary** | Diagnosed conditions | Reduce severity and recurrence |
| **Quaternary** | Over-treated patients | Avoid unnecessary medical intervention |

## System 2: Mrazek-Haggerty (1994) model
| Level | Target |
|---|---|
| **Universal** | All eligible individuals |
| **Selective** | Higher-than-average risk subgroups |
| **Indicated** | Individuals with detectable subclinical symptoms |

## Frieden's Pyramid
Most impactful interventions are structural (base of pyramid), least impactful are individual counseling (top).
