# Systematic Review

Aims to **identify, evaluate, and summarise** findings of all relevant individual studies on a specific topic.

## Characteristics
- Focused research question
- Defined reporting guidelines
- Specified data-extraction methodology
- Critical appraisal of each reviewed study
