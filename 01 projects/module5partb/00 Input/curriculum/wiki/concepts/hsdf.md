# Health Stigma and Discrimination Framework (HSDF)

Avoids the **stigmatiser-stigmatised dichotomy** in favour of **contextual factors**.

## Drivers
Factors that increase stigma — stereotypes, social/economic threats, fear of illness contagion.

## Facilitators
Factors that influence stigma (positively or negatively) — health policy, legislation, cultural norms.

**Drivers × Facilitators** determine whether a person is labelled and stigmatised. Also recognises [[intersectionality]] — labelling may depend on gender, age, race as well as the health condition.
