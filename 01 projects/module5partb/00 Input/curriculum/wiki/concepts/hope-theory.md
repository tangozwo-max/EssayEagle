# Hope Theory (C.R. Snyder)

Framework for how people conceptualise and use hope to achieve goals. Hope = perceived ability to produce pathways to achieve goals + motivation to use those pathways.

## Three components
1. **Goals** — the desired outcome.
2. **Pathways** — multiple routes to reach goals (cognitive / planning).
3. **Agency** — motivation and belief in capacity to initiate and sustain actions.

## Clinical application
Used in therapy for depression, anxiety, PTSD — helps rebuild agency, construct new pathways, set meaningful goals. Central to trauma recovery.
