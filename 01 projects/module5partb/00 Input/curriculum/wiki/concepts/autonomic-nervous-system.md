# Autonomic Nervous System (ANS)

Component of the PNS regulating **involuntary** physiological processes: heart rate, blood pressure, respiration, digestion, sexual arousal. Two balancing branches:

- [[sympathetic-parasympathetic-ns]] — the sympathetic NS (accelerator) and parasympathetic NS (brake).

Balance maintains homeostasis.
