# Optimism and Pessimism — Neural Correlates

Dr. Elaine Fox (University of Adelaide): each trait is rooted in competing motivations.

- **Optimism** — **nucleus accumbens**-driven; pursue pleasure.
- **Pessimism** — **amygdala**-driven; avoid pain.

Positive affirmations (Émile Coué's autosuggestion — "Every day, in every way, I'm getting better and better") can buffer stress and improve health **if aligned with core personal values**.

Positive expectancies without personal agency (pure optimism) less protective than those with agency (hope).
