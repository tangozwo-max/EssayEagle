# Sampling Methods (Qualitative Research)

| Method | Description | Caution |
|---|---|---|
| **Convenience** | Participants easily accessible to researcher | Representativeness questionable |
| **Purposive (judgment)** | Selected for specific study purpose with inclusion/exclusion criteria | Relies on researcher judgment |
| **Snowball** | Participants recruit further participants via peer networks | Useful for hard-to-access populations |
