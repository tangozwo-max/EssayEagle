# Trauma-Focused CBT (TF-CBT)

CBT adapted specifically for **processing traumatic experiences** and treating PTSD/CPTSD.

## When to use
Most effective when the person's **immediate life situation has stabilized** (less acute stress). Hassan's case: TF-CBT deferred until asylum situation settled.

## Components
- Trauma processing (gradual exposure to memories)
- Cognitive restructuring of trauma-related beliefs
- Safety and coping skills
- Parent/caregiver components (in child/youth versions)

## Evidence
- Van Dis et al. meta-analysis: CBT for anxiety disorders effective until 12 months. At >12 months, effects larger for PTSD than for GAD/SAD.
- Most effective long-term for PTSD compared to other anxiety disorders.
