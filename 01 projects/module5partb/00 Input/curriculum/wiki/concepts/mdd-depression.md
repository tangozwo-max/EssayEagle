# Major Depressive Disorder (MDD)

Also called clinical depression or unipolar depression.

## ICD-11 criteria
- Duration: ≥2 weeks
- ≥2 of 3 **core symptoms**: low mood, reduced energy, diminished interest
- Plus ≥2 **supplementary**: loss of self-esteem, guilt, suicidal thoughts, reduced concentration, agitation/retardation, sleep disturbance, appetite disturbance

## DSM-5 criteria
- ≥1 of 2 **core**: low mood OR loss of interest/pleasure
- Plus ≥4 **supplementary**: sleep change, appetite/weight change, reduced energy, poor concentration, guilt/worthlessness, agitation/retardation, suicidal ideas

## Severity
- **Mild**: minimal supplementary symptoms, function possible with effort
- **Moderate**: several supplementary, function affected
- **Severe**: most symptoms, minimal function

## Epidemiology
- 5% 12-month prevalence; 30-40% lifetime prevalence
- Most episodes remit within 1 year
- Highest disability of all disorders at societal level
