# Antagonist

Substance that **binds to a receptor without activating it**. Subtypes:
- **Competitive antagonists** — block NT binding at the primary binding site.
- **Non-competitive antagonists** — bind elsewhere (allosteric sites) or block the ion channel; reduce activation without competing with NT.

Ketamine is a non-competitive NMDA antagonist — channel blocker with allosteric binding.
