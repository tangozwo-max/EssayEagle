# Randomized Controlled Trial (RCT)

Quantitative study in which participants are randomly assigned to:
- **Experimental group** — receives the new intervention under study
- **Control / comparison group** — receives usual therapy, placebo, or alternative

Considered the **gold-standard** design for questions of **therapy effectiveness** ([[hierarchy-of-evidence]]).
