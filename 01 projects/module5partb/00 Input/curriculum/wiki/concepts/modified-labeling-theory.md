# Modified Labeling Theory (MLT)

Framework for stigma: majority-group members **label** minority-group members and devalue them for deviation from norm. Labels shape self-view and social behaviour.

## Socialisation process
Individuals are socialised into negative attitudes toward people with MH conditions. When a person acquires a condition, these attitudes become personally relevant → expectation of devaluation.

## Common responses of labeled individuals
- **Secrecy** — hiding symptoms/diagnosis
- **Withdrawal** — avoiding social situations
- **Education** — educating others

## Negative outcomes
Unemployment, low earnings, shame, guilt, social exclusion, demoralisation.
