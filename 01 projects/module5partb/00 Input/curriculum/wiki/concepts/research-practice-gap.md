# Research-Practice Gap

Disconnect between effects observed in peer-reviewed RCTs and the **real-world impact** on lived experiences of treatment-seeking people in the general population.

## Key drivers
- Clinical-trial populations poorly represent general population — e.g., Black Americans ~13% of US population but only ~5% of trial participants; Hispanic/Latino ~19% of population but ~1% of trial participants (Pfizer).
- Differences in what counts as "best evidence" by ethnicity, culture, gender identity, disability status.
- English-language bias in systematic reviews.

> **Caution:** No universal applicability assumption should be made when applying evidence across demographics.
