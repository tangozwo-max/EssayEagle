# Complex PTSD (C-PTSD)

Extends PTSD (re-experiencing, avoidance, hyperarousal) with:
- Persistent **problems in emotion regulation**
- Beliefs of being **"worthless"** or **"defeated"**
- Disturbed relationships / sense of self

Often associated with **prolonged, repeated, or interpersonal trauma** (childhood abuse, domestic violence, captivity).
