# Watchful Waiting

A period of **active monitoring without immediate treatment**. Recommended for mild MH conditions where spontaneous remission is likely.

## Purpose
- Further assessment
- Relationship building
- Shared decision-making
- Many mild anxiety/depression symptoms improve spontaneously after an empathetic consultation

## During watchful waiting
- Recommend psychoeducation
- Lifestyle interventions, particularly **exercise**
- Follow up to determine if symptoms persist/worsen

## When to escalate
If symptoms persist, worsen, or significantly impair functioning → move to brief psychological treatment or medication.
