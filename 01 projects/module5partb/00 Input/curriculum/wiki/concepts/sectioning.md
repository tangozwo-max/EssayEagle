# Sectioning

Colloquial term for **involuntary admission under the Mental Health Act (UK)** — named after the different *sections* of the Act that prescribe grounds for admission. Requires assessment by **three qualified professionals**.

## Common sections
- **Section 2** — assessment for up to 28 days
- **Section 3** — treatment for up to 6 months (renewable)
- **Section 4** — emergency, up to 72 hours
- **Section 136** — police detention from public place for up to 24 hours
