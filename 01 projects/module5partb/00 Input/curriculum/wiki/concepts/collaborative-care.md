# Collaborative Care

NICE-recommended **multi-disciplinary treatment model** for mild-to-moderate depression when the person also has a chronic physical health condition and difficulty with daily tasks.

## MDT components
- **Care Manager**: main contact; monitors symptoms, supports medication adherence, provides PST
- **Physician**: diagnosis, treatment planning, prescribing
- **Psychiatrist**: supervision, consultation, treatment adjustment

## Evidence: IMPACT Study
- Demonstrated effectiveness for depression in primary care (older adults with arthritis, depression)
- Reduced depressive symptoms + decreased pain + improved functional status and quality of life
- For diabetes comorbidity: limited effect on glycemic control — may need tailored self-care additions
