# Adult-Born Immature Granule Neurons (ABINs)

Immature neurons generated in the **hippocampal dentate gyrus** via adult neurogenesis. Rawat et al.: ketamine may stimulate ABIN production; blocking ABIN activity abolishes ketamine's antidepressant effect; activating ABINs alone replicates the effect.
