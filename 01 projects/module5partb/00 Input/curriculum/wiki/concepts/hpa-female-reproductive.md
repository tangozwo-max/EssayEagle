# HPA Axis and the Female Reproductive Lifecycle

Women report higher stress levels than men; internalise stress (→ physical and mental conditions) while men externalise (aggression, impulsivity).

## Key findings (Hantsoo et al. 2023)
- Ovarian hormones (estrogen, progesterone) interact with the HPA axis.
- **Estradiol** acts as an inhibitor of HPA stress response.
- Depression at different reproductive stages has different HPA associations.
- PMDD, postpartum depression, perimenopausal depression all involve HPA dysregulation.
- Perimenopausal women (40–50): **~7× increased risk of suicidal ideation** vs other groups.
