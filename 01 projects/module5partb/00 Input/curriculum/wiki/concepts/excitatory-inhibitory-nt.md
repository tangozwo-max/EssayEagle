# Excitatory vs Inhibitory Neurotransmitters

- **Excitatory** — *increase* likelihood that the postsynaptic neuron fires an action potential. Main excitatory NT: **glutamate**.
- **Inhibitory** — *decrease* likelihood of firing. Main inhibitory NT: **GABA**.

Balance of hundreds of excitatory and inhibitory inputs determines whether an action potential fires.
