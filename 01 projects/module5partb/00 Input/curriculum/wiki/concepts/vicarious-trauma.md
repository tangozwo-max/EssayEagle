# Vicarious Trauma

A **transformation of the helper's inner world** resulting from empathic engagement with traumatised individuals. Distinct from burnout (job exhaustion) — vicarious trauma specifically alters beliefs, assumptions, and worldview.

Also called **secondary traumatisation** or **compassion fatigue**, though these terms have subtle differences.

## Symptoms
- Intrusive imagery from clients' accounts
- Disrupted beliefs about safety, trust, and control
- Emotional numbing or over-involvement
- Blurred professional boundaries
- Physical and cognitive exhaustion

## Protective factors
- Supervision and peer support
- Caseload diversification
- Self-care practices
- Organisational policies limiting exposure
