# Journal Clubs

Peer-learning format for disseminating research findings. First formal journal club initiated by **Sir William Osler** at McMaster University (Canada) in 1877.

## Selection criteria for articles
- High rejection-rate journals (quality proxy)
- Recent publication date
- Study design appropriate to topic
- Strong author affiliations / funding bodies
- Innovative methods or important findings (including negative findings)

## Function
- Shared critical appraisal using structured tools
- Dissemination of new evidence
- Development of appraisal skills
- Networking, mentoring
