# Child Maltreatment

Abuse and neglect of children, including physical abuse, sexual abuse, emotional abuse, and neglect. Represents a major adverse childhood experience with severe consequences for mental and physical health.

## Impact on mental health

- Directly causes psychological trauma and PTSD
- Associated with depression, anxiety, behavioral problems
- Nearly 10 times more likely to have multiple neurodevelopmental conditions
- Creates "double jeopardy" when combined with pre-existing NDCs

## Clinical considerations

- Full neurodevelopmental assessment recommended for all maltreated children
- Children with multiple NDCs are at higher risk of maltreatment (vulnerability)
- Trauma effects (hypervigilance, emotional dysregulation) may mask or delay NDC diagnosis
