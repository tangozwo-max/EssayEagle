# Hospital Anxiety and Depression Scale (HADS)

14-item scale: **7 items anxiety + 7 items depression**. Designed for patients with physical illness comorbidities (hospital context). Respondents rate past week.

## Scoring (per section, 0-21)
| Score | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| 0-7 | Normal |
| 8-10 | Borderline abnormal |
| 11-14 | Abnormal |
| 15+ | Severe anxiety or depression |

## Uses
- Screening in medical settings
- Monitoring treatment response
- Dual screening for both anxiety and depression
