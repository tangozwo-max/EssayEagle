# Poverty

Economic deprivation characterized by insufficient income and resources to meet basic needs. Poverty represents a major social determinant of health and mental health, particularly impacting child development and wellbeing.

## Related factors

- **Food insecurity** — unreliable access to adequate nutrition
- **Housing instability** — unstable, inadequate, or unaffordable housing
- **Educational deprivation** — limited access to quality schooling
- **Healthcare access** — reduced access to preventive and treatment services
- **Social deprivation** — reduced access to community resources and opportunities

## Impact on children

Poverty affects multiple systems that influence child mental health:
- Parental stress and mental health (reducing parenting capacity)
- Nutritional status (affecting brain development)
- Stress exposure (chronic toxic stress)
- Educational opportunities (affecting cognitive and social development)
- Social connections (limiting protective relationships)
