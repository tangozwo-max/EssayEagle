# Ancient Mental Health Theories

In ancient civilisations, mental disorders were believed to arise from **imbalances in life forces**.

| Civilisation | Theory | Treatments |
|---|---|---|
| **Traditional Chinese Medicine** | Imbalance of yin and yang | Herbal potions, acupuncture |
| **Ancient Greece (Hippocrates)** | Imbalance of four humours (blood, phlegm, black bile, yellow bile) | Diet, exercise, purging |
| **Ancient Egypt** | Spiritual possession; illness located in body organs | Incantations, herbs, rituals |
| **Ancient Rome (Galen)** | Extended humoural theory; body fluids and ventricles | Animal dissection informed anatomy |
