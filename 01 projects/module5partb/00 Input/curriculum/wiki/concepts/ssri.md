# SSRI — Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitor

One of the most commonly prescribed classes of antidepressants. Increase serotonin levels in the brain by **blocking its reabsorption** at the synapse. Widely used despite ongoing debate about the [[monoamine-deficiency-hypothesis]].
