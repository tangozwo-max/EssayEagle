# Cultural Norms and Mental Health

Cultural norms vary significantly across regions, nations, and communities. Crucial to understand how people from different cultural backgrounds think and talk about mental-health issues.

- Some cultures frame MH conditions in spiritual / mystical terms (e.g., Gabon case study).
- Stigma varies dramatically across cultures.
- Language use, help-seeking behaviour, and acceptable coping strategies are culturally specific.
- "Western" interventions may need adaptation for other cultures.
