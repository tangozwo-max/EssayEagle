# Fear vs Anxiety

| Fear | Anxiety |
|---|---|
| Response to **imminent** threat | **Future-oriented anticipation** |
| Present, identifiable trigger | Trigger vague or absent |
| Activates fight-or-flight | Persistent worry / arousal |

Clinically important distinction during assessment — informs intervention choice (exposure vs cognitive restructuring).
