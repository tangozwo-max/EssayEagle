# Stereotype · Prejudice · Discrimination (the stigma triad)

| Element | Psychological mode | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Stereotype** | Cognition | "People with schizophrenia are dangerous." |
| **Prejudice** | Emotion | "Yes, they are dangerous — I feel uncomfortable around them." |
| **Discrimination** | Behaviour | "I won't hire someone like that." |

Stigma is created via a **cognition → emotion → behaviour** chain.
