# Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)

**Third-wave CBT** therapy. Prioritizes holistic promotion of psychological/behavioral health over reduction of symptoms.

## Core approach
- **Stop avoiding, denying, struggling** with inner emotions
- **Accept** that difficult feelings are appropriate responses to certain situations
- Those feelings should NOT prevent you from **moving forward in life**
- **Commit** to making necessary behavioral changes, regardless of feelings

## Mechanism
- Mindfulness + values clarification + committed action
- Psychological flexibility as the key outcome
- ACT matrix: toward vs. away moves; values vs. fears

## Evidence
Effective for GAD, depression, chronic pain. Recommended for Victor (M2 W6 case study) with multiple life domains causing anxiety.
