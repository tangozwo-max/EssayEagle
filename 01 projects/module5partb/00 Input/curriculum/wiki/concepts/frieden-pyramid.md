# Frieden's Public Health Impact Pyramid

A framework describing the relative population impact of different public health interventions. Lower = broader impact.

```
Level 5 (least impact): Counseling and education — individual behavior change
Level 4: Clinical care — medical treatment
Level 3: Long-term protective interventions — e.g., early childhood, resilience training
Level 2: Environment/context changes — workplace policy, stigma reduction
Level 1 (most impact): Socioeconomic factors — poverty, education, housing
```

## For mental health
- Base: poverty reduction, gender equity, universal basic income
- Level 2: workplace MH policies, inclusive schools, anti-stigma
- Level 3: trauma-informed care, resilience programs
- Level 4: psychotherapy, medication
- Level 5: psychoeducation sessions

Structural interventions (base) are most **effective, rapid, equitable, cost-effective, and sustainable**.
