# Mental Health First Aid (MHFA)

Widely available **general-education** intervention that trains lay people and professionals to recognise MH symptoms, offer initial help, and guide toward professional care.

## Aims
- Improve MH literacy
- Improve awareness of MH importance
- Improve symptom recognition and early identification
- Encourage help-seeking
