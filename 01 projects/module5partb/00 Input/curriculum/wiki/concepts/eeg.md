# Electroencephalography (EEG)

Scalp electrodes record tiny voltage fluctuations from neuronal activity as **brainwaves**, categorised by frequency (delta, theta, alpha, beta, gamma). Non-invasive (can be invasive with internal electrodes). First human EEG: Hans Berger 1924.

## Uses
- Seizure diagnosis and localisation
- Sleep-disorder assessment
- Encephalopathies
- Brain-death determination

## Strengths / limitations
High **temporal** resolution; low **spatial** resolution.
