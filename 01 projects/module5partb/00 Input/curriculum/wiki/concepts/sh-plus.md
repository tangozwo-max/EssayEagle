# SH+ — Self-Help Plus

**WHO group-based stress management** intervention. Used in humanitarian settings.

## Format
- 5 sessions for groups of up to 30 people
- Delivered by supervised nonspecialist facilitators
- Uses prerecorded audio + illustrated guide (bibliotherapy)
- Minimal expert time required

## Evidence
Reduces psychological distress and **prevents onset of mental disorders** in adults experiencing stress regardless of circumstances.
