# Adjustment Disorder

**ICD-11 stress-related disorder**: maladaptive reaction to an identifiable stressor (job loss, relationship breakdown, bereavement), not meeting criteria for PTSD or CPTSD.

## Features
- Preoccupation with the stressor
- Inability to adapt
- Onset within 1 month of stressor
- Typically resolves within 6 months when stressor ends

## Differential from PTSD/CPTSD
- Less severe and less persistent
- Does not require traumatic stressor (any significant stressor)
- PTSD requires re-experiencing, avoidance, hyperarousal
