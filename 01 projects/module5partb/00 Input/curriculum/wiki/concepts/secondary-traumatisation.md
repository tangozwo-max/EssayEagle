# Secondary Traumatisation

Effect on helpers of being exposed to clients' trauma stories. Can produce symptoms similar to PTSD — nightmares, emotional numbing, hypervigilance. Overlaps with [[vicarious-trauma]] and [[moral-distress]].

## Protection
- [[balint-groups]] / [[schwartz-rounds]]
- [[trim-trauma-risk-management]]
- Clinical supervision, buddy system, safe spaces
