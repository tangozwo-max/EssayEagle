# Behavioral Activation (BA)

CBT technique that helps people regain lost or reduced functioning in daily activities. Particularly effective for depression.

## 5 Steps (TalkPlus workbook)
1. Keep a **baseline diary** of current activities
2. **Identify** routine, pleasurable, and necessary activities not currently done
3. **Rank** activities as easy, moderate, or difficult
4. **Schedule** activities (activity timetables)
5. **Implement and monitor**

## Mechanism
- Breaks the depression cycle of inactivity → reduced positive reinforcement → more depression
- Treats timetable entries as "appointments with oneself"
- Starts with short-term goals; gradually increases

## Evidence
Strong evidence base for moderate depression. Was the most recommended intervention for Isabella (case study) in M2 W6.
