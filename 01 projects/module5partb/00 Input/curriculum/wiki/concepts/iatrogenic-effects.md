# Iatrogenic Effects

**Unintended harm caused by medical treatment**. From Greek: *iatros* = physician, *genesis* = origin.

## Relevance in comorbid MH/physical illness
- Tricyclic antidepressants (TCAs) + ischaemic heart disease → cardiac interactions
- SSRIs + diabetes → interactions affecting glycemic control
- Polypharmacy in comorbid patients significantly increases risk

## Drug interactions to be aware of
- SSRIs + warfarin/NSAIDs → increased bleeding risk
- TCAs: avoid in cardiovascular disease, seizures, hyperthyroidism, urinary retention
