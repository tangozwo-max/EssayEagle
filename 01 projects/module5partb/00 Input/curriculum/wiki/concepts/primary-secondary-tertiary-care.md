# Primary / Secondary / Tertiary Mental Health Care

| Level | Scope | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary** | Initial assessment, mild-moderate conditions, medication for common conditions (depression, anxiety), physical health checks for SMI | Family doctors, nurse practitioners, social-prescribing link workers |
| **Secondary (community)** | Complex needs, MDT support | Community Mental Health Teams, crisis/home-treatment teams, perinatal MH, early-intervention-in-psychosis |
| **Tertiary** | Severe and enduring conditions; highly specialised treatment | Psychiatric hospitals, specialist units, inpatient care, day programmes, child/adolescent psychiatry |

## Integration goals (WHO, NHS Long Term Plan)
Community-based, person-centred, human-rights-based; integrate with primary care, housing, employment, education, social protection.
