# Two-Stage Capacity Test (UK MCA)

- **Stage 1** — Is there an impairment of, or disturbance in, the functioning of mind or brain?
- **Stage 2** — Is the impairment sufficient that the person cannot make *this particular* decision at *this particular* time?

## Four criteria for making a decision
A person has capacity if they can:
1. **Understand** information relevant to the decision
2. **Remember** it long enough to decide
3. **Weigh up** the information
4. **Communicate** the decision (talking, sign language, any means)
