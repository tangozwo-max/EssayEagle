# Informed Consent

Process by which a person receives all relevant information about a treatment, medication, or research study — enabling them to make an **autonomous decision**. Practical application of the [[four-principles-bioethics]] principle of respect for persons / autonomy.

## Challenges in mental healthcare
- Capacity may fluctuate (delusions, acute psychosis, cognitive impairment).
- ECT consent debated — critics argue risks/benefits not fully conveyed.
- Novel technologies (neuromodulation, AI) hard to explain in lay terms.
