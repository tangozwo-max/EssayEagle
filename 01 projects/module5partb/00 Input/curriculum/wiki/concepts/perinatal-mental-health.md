# Perinatal / Postpartum Mental Health

Mental-health conditions occurring during pregnancy (perinatal) or after childbirth (postpartum/postnatal). Includes depression, anxiety, PTSD, psychosis.

- Intimate-partner abuse during pregnancy strongly linked to postnatal depression/anxiety/PTSD (Lund et al.).
- CMHTs may have specialist perinatal teams.
