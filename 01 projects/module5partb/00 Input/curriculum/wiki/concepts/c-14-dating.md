# Carbon-14 Dating of Neurons

Method enabled by the atmospheric surge of radioactive **C-14** from mid-20th-century atomic bomb testing. C-14 entered the food chain → incorporated into DNA of dividing cells. Measuring C-14 levels in hippocampal neuron DNA dates each cell.

Used by Spalding et al. (2013) to confirm ongoing adult neurogenesis in humans — a landmark discovery reshaping understanding of brain regeneration.
