# Social Exclusion

A complex, dynamic set of processes and relationships that prevent individuals or groups from:
- Accessing resources
- Participating in society
- Asserting their rights

Results in exclusion from social, political, and economic institutions. Often compounds with stigma, discrimination, and intersectional disadvantage.
