# Reliability, Validity, Relevance, Applicability

Four quality dimensions for appraising quantitative research.

| Dimension | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Reliability** | Same method, similar conditions → similar results. Reproducible across researchers. |
| **Validity** | Does the study measure what it claims? Subtypes: **internal** (accurate answer to question) and **external** (generalisability to other populations/contexts). |
| **Relevance** | Do results directly contribute to understanding the condition/intervention? Practical implications for appraiser and person. |
| **Applicability** | Can findings be implemented in your context? Considers population features and feasibility barriers. |
