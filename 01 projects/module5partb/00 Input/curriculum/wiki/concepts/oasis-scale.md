# OASIS — Overall Anxiety Severity and Impairment Scale

A 5-item transdiagnostic self-report scale measuring anxiety severity and functional impairment over the past week. Items cover:
1. Frequency of anxiety
2. Intensity of anxiety
3. Avoidance
4. Interference at work/school/home
5. Interference in social relationships

Each item scored 0–4; total 0–20. **Cut-off ≥ 8** suggests clinically significant anxiety. Validated across anxiety disorders and suitable for monitoring treatment response.
