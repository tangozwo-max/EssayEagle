# Mixed Anxiety and Depressive Disorder

**ICD-11 diagnosis**: co-existing symptoms of anxiety AND depression where **neither set, considered separately, is sufficiently severe, numerous, or persistent** to justify a diagnosis of a depressive disorder or anxiety disorder individually.

## Distinguished from
- **GAD**: in mixed disorder, anxiety symptoms do not meet full GAD criteria
- **MDD**: depressive symptoms do not meet full criteria
- **Anxious depression** (proposed): when both DO meet case-level criteria
