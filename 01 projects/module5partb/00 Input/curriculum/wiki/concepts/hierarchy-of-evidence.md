# Hierarchy of Evidence by Question Type

Different research questions call for different optimal study designs (> means "stronger than").

| Area | Question | Optimal study |
|---|---|---|
| Therapy | Is this intervention effective? | **RCT** > cohort > case control |
| Diagnosis | How accurate is this test? | Cross-sectional or cohort > case control |
| Prognosis | What happens over time? | Cohort > case series with follow-up |
| Etiology | What causes this condition? | Cohort (common) or case control (rare) > cross-sectional |
| Frequency | How common is it? | Cross-sectional or cohort |
| Experience | What is it like? | **Qualitative** |
