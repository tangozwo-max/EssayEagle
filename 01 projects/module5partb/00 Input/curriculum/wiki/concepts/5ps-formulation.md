# 5Ps Model of Case Formulation

The 5Ps model is a CBT-derived framework for developing a structured formulation from assessment data. It organises factors into five categories: **Presenting difficulties** (current symptoms, thoughts, feelings, behaviours), **Predisposing factors** (biological, genetic, environmental, psychological vulnerabilities), **Precipitating factors** (triggers immediately preceding onset or worsening), **Perpetuating factors** (dynamics maintaining or worsening the condition), and **Protective/positive factors** (strengths and supports mitigating impact). Originally developed for adult CBT, it is widely used in CAMH to structure holistic case understanding.
