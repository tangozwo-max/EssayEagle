# Magnetoencephalography (MEG)

Measures **magnetic** activity of neurons (complementary to EEG's electrical signals). Uses **SQUID** (Superconducting Quantum Interference Device) sensors in a magnetically shielded room. First signals: David Cohen 1960s; SQUID by James Zimmerman.

Like EEG: high temporal, lower spatial resolution.
