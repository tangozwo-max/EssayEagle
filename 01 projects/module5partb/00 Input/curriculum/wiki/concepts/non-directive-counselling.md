# Non-Directive Supportive Counselling

Also known as **person-centered** or **client-centered** counselling. Based on the work of **Carl Rogers**.

## Three Core Conditions (Rogers)
1. **Nonjudgmental empathy** — genuine understanding of the client's perspective
2. **Congruence** — therapist's genuineness, including body language
3. **Unconditional positive regard** — accepting the client without judgment

## Approach
- Service user takes **active role** in defining and gaining insight into problems
- Therapist does NOT have the answers
- Service user is encouraged to grow toward maturity and adjustment

## Evidence (Cuijpers et al.)
Non-directive supportive counseling was NOT more efficacious than pill placebo in meta-analysis — but is still widely used as a component of care.
