# Associative Learning

Formation of an association between two stimuli, or between a behaviour and a stimulus. Example: an infant learning that a parent leaving the room to answer the doorbell does not mean permanent abandonment.

Central mechanism in childhood neurodevelopment — drives strengthening of frequently used neural connections.
