# Five Ways to Wellbeing

Evidence-based framework for promoting wellbeing. Recommended for mild symptoms of anxiety or depression. [Aked et al., New Economics Foundation]

## Five Actions
1. **Connect** — strengthen relationships with people around you
2. **Be active** — find physical activity you enjoy
3. **Take notice** — be curious, catch sight of the beautiful, remark on the unusual
4. **Keep learning** — try something new, rediscover old interests
5. **Give** — do something kind for a friend or a stranger

## Evidence base
Developed from review of research on wellbeing factors. Used widely in primary care psychoeducation.
