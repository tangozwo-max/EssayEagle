# Seasonal Affective Disorder (SAD)

*Note: SAD also = Social Anxiety Disorder — different concept.*

Subtype of recurrent major depression with **seasonal pattern** (specifier in DSM-5/ICD-11). Typically:
- Onset: autumn/winter
- Remission: spring/summer

## Mechanism
Linked to reduced light exposure → altered circadian rhythm, reduced serotonin, elevated melatonin.

## Treatment
- **Light therapy** (bright light 10,000 lux, 30 min/morning)
- CBT
- SSRIs (typically same as MDD)
