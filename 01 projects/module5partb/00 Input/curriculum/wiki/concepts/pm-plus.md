# PM+ — Problem Management Plus

**WHO transdiagnostic psychological intervention** for adults dealing with distress in communities exposed to adversity. Delivered by lay therapists.

## Format
5 weekly sessions, individual or group

## Components
1. Psychoeducation
2. Managing stress (breathing, relaxation)
3. Problem-solving therapy (PST)
4. Behavioral activation
5. Strengthening social support

## Evidence
Effective for depression, anxiety, and stress regardless of whether adversity caused the problems. Specifically designed for resource-limited settings.

## Note
Deliberately does not use MH terminology — focuses on "problems of living."
