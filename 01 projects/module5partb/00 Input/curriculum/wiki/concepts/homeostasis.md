# Homeostasis

"A state of balance among all the body systems needed for the body to survive and function correctly." Dynamic self-regulation maintaining stable internal environment despite external change. Term coined by **Walter Cannon (1926)**; concept rooted in Claude Bernard's **milieu intérieur**.

## Three-part mechanism
- **Receptor** — detects stimulus / stressor
- **Control centre** — integrates, decides response (e.g., hypothalamus)
- **Effector** — executes response (e.g., adrenal gland releasing cortisol)

Regulated variables: blood pressure, blood sugar, electrolytes, energy, hormones, oxygen, proteins, temperature.
