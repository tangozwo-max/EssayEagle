# Bipolar Disorder

Mood disorder with alternating episodes of mania (or hypomania) and depression.

## Types
- **Bipolar I** — full manic episodes, often with depressive episodes.
- **Bipolar II** — hypomanic + depressive episodes (no full mania).
- **Cyclothymia** — milder chronic mood swings.

## Management
Mood stabilisers (lithium), antipsychotics; care often tertiary level (e.g., MaHTAS guidelines in Malaysia).
