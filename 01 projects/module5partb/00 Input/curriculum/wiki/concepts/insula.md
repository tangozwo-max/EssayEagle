# Insula (insular cortex)

Small region deep within the lateral fissure. Integrates sensory, cognitive, emotional processes. Anterior insula is a hub of the [[salience-network]].

## Clinical links
Hyperactivity or dysfunction → anxiety, depression, hallucinations, emotional dysregulation, inability to resist addictive behaviour.
