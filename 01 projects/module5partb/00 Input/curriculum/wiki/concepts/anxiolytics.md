# Anxiolytics

Medications used to **reduce anxiety**. Main classes:

| Class | Examples | Mechanism | Notes |
|-------|----------|-----------|-------|
| SSRIs/SNRIs | Sertraline, Venlafaxine | Serotonin/noradrenaline reuptake inhibition | First-line for chronic anxiety |
| Benzodiazepines | Diazepam, Lorazepam | GABA-A potentiation | Effective short-term; risk of dependence with prolonged use |
| Buspirone | Buspirone | 5-HT1A partial agonist | Non-sedating, non-addictive; slow onset |
| Beta-blockers | Propranolol | β-adrenergic blockade | Targets physical symptoms (palpitations) |
| Hydroxyzine | Hydroxyzine | Antihistamine | Short-term, non-addictive |
