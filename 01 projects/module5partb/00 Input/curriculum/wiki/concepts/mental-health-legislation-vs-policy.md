# Mental Health Legislation vs Policy

| Aspect | **Legislation** | **Policy** |
|---|---|---|
| Definition | Laws and regulations governing assessment, treatment, rights | Strategic guidelines and principles |
| Focus | Legal frameworks — admission, detention, treatment | Prevention, promotion, overall well-being |
| Components | Involuntary admission criteria, rights, appeal | Service provision, funding, access, quality |
| Example | UK Mental Health Act 1983/2007; Malaysia MHA 2001 | KSA Vision 2030 holistic care policy |

## WHO legislation principles
- Minimise restrictive options / maximise independence
- Empowerment and involvement
- Respect and dignity
- Purpose and effectiveness
- Efficiency and equity
