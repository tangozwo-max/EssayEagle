# Panic Disorder

Recurrent unexpected panic attacks (sudden surges of intense fear with physical symptoms — racing heart, sweating, dizziness, shortness of breath, feeling of dying/"going mad") + persistent concern or behaviour change related to attacks.

Often relates to misinterpretation of the [[fight-or-flight]] response.
