# Euthanasia for Psychiatric Conditions

Controversial practice where legal euthanasia eligibility extends to people with mental-health conditions whose suffering is deemed unbearable. Raises concerns around **erosion of nonmaleficence** and contributes to [[moral-distress]] among care staff.

## Countries permitting euthanasia for non-terminal psychiatric conditions (2024)
- Netherlands
- Switzerland
- Germany
- Canada
- Spain
- Australia

Requirements vary widely across jurisdictions.

## Case referenced
Jolanda Fun, 34, Netherlands — eating disorder, recurrent depression, autism; euthanasia 2024.
