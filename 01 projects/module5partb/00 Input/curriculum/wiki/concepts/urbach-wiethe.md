# Urbach-Wiethe Disease (SM case)

Rare genetic disorder causing selective damage to the **amygdala**. The case of "SM" demonstrated the amygdala's specific role in fear — SM was essentially fearless despite surviving a knifepoint robbery and handling spiders and snakes.
