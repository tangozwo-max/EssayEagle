# Epigenetics of Stress

Changes in **gene expression** without altering the genetic code, triggered by experience. Animal studies: nurturing-mother rat pups develop more cortisol receptors → less stress sensitivity. Neglected pups show opposite outcome — lifelong increased stress sensitivity.

Suggests stress response is **heritable via epigenetic mechanisms**, especially when exposure occurs in early life.
