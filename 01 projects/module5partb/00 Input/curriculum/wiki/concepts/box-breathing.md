# Box Breathing / 4-7-8 Breathing

Slow-breathing techniques to trigger the parasympathetic nervous system.

- **Box breathing (4-4-4-4)**: inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 4s, hold 4s.
- **4-7-8**: inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale 8s.

Used for anxiety, panic, stress management.
