# Adultification

Adultification is a form of bias where children from Black, Asian, and minoritised ethnic communities are perceived as more "streetwise," mature, and less vulnerable than white children of the same age. This particularly affects Black children, who may be viewed as threatening rather than needing protection. Consequences: assumptions that children can protect themselves (e.g., Black boys involved in gangs), failure to safeguard from exploitation and violence, under-reporting of abuse, and overrepresentation in the youth justice system rather than child protection systems. Practitioners must recognise and counteract this bias through culturally sensitive, strength-based approaches.
