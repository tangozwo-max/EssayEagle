# Bipolar Spectrum

Mood disorders characterized by alternating episodes of depression and elevated mood (mania/hypomania).

## Types
| Type | Features |
|---|---|
| **Bipolar I** | Full mania + depressive episodes. Mania: very high mood, overactive, may last weeks |
| **Bipolar II** | Hypomania (less severe) + depressive episodes |
| **Cyclothymia** | Sub-threshold alternating moods, chronic |
| **Bipolar NOS** | Subthreshold, not otherwise specified |

## Epidemiology
- Bipolar I lifetime prevalence: ~1%
- Bipolar spectrum: ~2.4% (11-country study)

## Neuroscience
Lithium stabilizes mood; investigated for neurogenesis/BDNF effects. Serotonin, noradrenaline, dopamine involved.
