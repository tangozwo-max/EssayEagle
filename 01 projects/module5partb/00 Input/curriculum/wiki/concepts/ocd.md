# Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)

Recurring unwanted obsessions and/or repetitive compulsions that the person feels driven to perform. In DSM-5 classified under "Obsessive-Compulsive and Related Disorders" (not Anxiety Disorders).

Common treatments: **Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP)**, CBT, SSRIs, TMS.
