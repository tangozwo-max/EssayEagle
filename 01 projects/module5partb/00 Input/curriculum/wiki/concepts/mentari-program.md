# Mentari Program (Malaysia)

National programme launched in 2014 by the Malaysian Ministry of Health to extend specialist psychiatric services from hospitals into the community.

- Central national office: MENTARI Selayang
- 30 community-based branches across all states (as of July 2021)
- Multidisciplinary and collaborative
- Shift from curative (hospital) → prevention and reintegration (community)
