# Mental Illness Stigma Framework (MISF)

Differentiates **perceived stigma** (cultural-level, marginalising identity) from **personal stigma** (individual's own stigmatising beliefs). Views both stigmatiser and stigmatised.

## Stigmatiser side
Creates/perpetuates stereotypes, prejudice, discrimination → reduced MH funding, limited employment.

## Stigmatised side
Internalised + experienced + anticipated stigma → treatment-engagement issues, wellness problems, lack of social support, poor physical health.

[[intersectionality]] further complicates the impact.
