# Contact-Based Education

Intervention based on **direct interaction** between members of the public and people who have experienced (and managed) MH conditions. Active discussion is facilitated.

## Evidence
- Improves knowledge.
- Reduces negative attitudes.
- Minimises self-reported social exclusion.
- Documented efficacy in schools and colleges.
- **Underused** in healthcare professional training (Patten et al. pharmacy-student study showed meaningful stigma reduction).

## Key requirement
PWLE involved should be in **recovery**.
