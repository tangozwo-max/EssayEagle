# CRPD — UN Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities

UN treaty ratified by **164 countries**. Protects rights of people with disabilities. **Prohibits detention and treatment without consent** — at odds with most modern mental-health legislation allowing involuntary detention under strict conditions.
