# Macro / Meso / Micro Levels

Ecological framework for where interventions act.

| Level | Scope | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Macro** | International, national, regional | Nationwide anti-stigma campaign, WHO action plan |
| **Meso** | Institutional, local government, organisational, community | Unconscious-bias training for staff, local council policy |
| **Micro** | Personal, familial | One-to-one psychoeducation; family-level support |

Commonly used for stigma interventions and for social-determinants work.
