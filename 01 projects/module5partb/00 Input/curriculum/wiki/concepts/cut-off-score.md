# Cut-off Score

Threshold score on a screening instrument that indicates a **positive** result warranting further assessment. Positive scores do not diagnose — they trigger deeper evaluation. Negative scores don't rule out symptoms worth intervention.

Examples:
- PHQ-9: ≥10 (moderate depression)
- GAD-7: 5/10/15 (mild/moderate/severe)
- PCL-5: 31–33 (provisional PTSD)
