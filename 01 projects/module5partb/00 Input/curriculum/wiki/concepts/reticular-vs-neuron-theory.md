# Reticular vs Neuron Theory

Late-19th / early-20th century theoretical debate.

- **Reticular theory** (Joseph von Gerlach) — nervous system is a single, continuous interconnected network.
- **Neuron theory** (Santiago Ramón y Cajal) — nervous system is made of discrete neurons communicating via synapses.

Cajal and Camillo Golgi shared the 1906 Nobel Prize while disagreeing publicly. Resolved in the **1950s by electron microscopy** — neuron theory was correct.
