# Membrane Potential

Electrical potential across a neuron's cell membrane, arising from uneven distribution of charged ions (Na⁺, K⁺, Cl⁻, Ca²⁺) inside vs outside.

- **Resting potential** — typically ~-60 to -70 mV.
- **Threshold** — ~-50 to -55 mV; triggers [[action-potential]].
- Expressed *inside-relative-to-outside*.
