# Neuromodulators

Substances that modulate neuronal and circuit function over **longer timescales and more diffuse areas** than neurotransmitters. Can tune entire brain regions, not just individual neurons. Serotonin, dopamine, and noradrenaline can act as neurotransmitters *and* as neuromodulators.
