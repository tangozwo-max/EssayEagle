# Oedipus Complex and Little Hans

**Little Hans** — Freud's 1909 case study of a 5-year-old boy with phobia of horses. Freud interpreted the fear through the **Oedipus complex** — the boy's unconscious erotic attachment to his mother and rivalry with his father.

## Critique
- Alternative explanations exist that don't rely on Freud's theories (e.g., classical conditioning via a frightening horse encounter).
- Demonstrates how theoretical assumptions shape interpretation of clinical observations.
