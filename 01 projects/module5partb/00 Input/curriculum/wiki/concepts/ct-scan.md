# CT — Computed Tomography

Rotating X-ray machine + computers → cross-sectional images. Different tissues absorb X-rays at different rates.

## Uses in neuroimaging
- Stroke (NCCT rapid initial scan)
- Tumours, lesions, injuries
- Intracranial bleeding
- Hydrocephalus
- Vascular dementia
- Infection / inflammation

## Variants
- **NCCT** — Non-contrast CT
- **CTA** — CT angiography (blood vessels)
- **CTP** — CT perfusion (blood-flow assessment)
