# Behaviourism

School of psychology advanced by **John B. Watson** (1878–1958) arguing that behaviour is **learned through conditioning**. Built on [[classical-conditioning]] (Pavlov). Rejected introspection; focused on observable behaviour.

## Implication for mental disorders
Mental-health conditions (phobias, anxiety) are acquired through conditioning and can be **unlearned** via new learning — foundation for exposure therapy and CBT behavioural techniques.
