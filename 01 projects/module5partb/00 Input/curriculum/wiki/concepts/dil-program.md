# DIL — Depression in Later Life Programme

An evidence-based **indicated prevention** programme targeting older adults (65+) with subclinical depressive symptoms to prevent progression to clinical depression.

Combines problem-solving therapy, behavioural activation, and social engagement strategies. Delivered in primary care or community settings. Developed in the Netherlands; subsequently adapted internationally.

Demonstrates that even brief, structured interventions can halve depression incidence in at-risk older populations.
