# UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

A collection of **17 interconnected global goals** adopted by the United Nations to achieve a better and more sustainable future by **2030**. Cover social, economic, and environmental challenges.

## Relevance to mental health — Goal 3
**SDG 3** ("Good Health and Well-being") explicitly emphasises inclusion of mental health care in **universal health coverage**.

Why this matters:
- Unipolar depressive disorders projected to be the **leading cause of disease burden** in HICs by 2030, and #2–#3 in MICs/LICs.
- Inclusion in SDG 3 anchors mental health in global policy financing discussions.

## Broader role
SDGs shape how funders, governments, and NGOs prioritise:
- Poverty (SDG 1), hunger (SDG 2), education (SDG 4), gender equality (SDG 5), clean water (SDG 6), decent work (SDG 8), inequality (SDG 10), peaceful societies (SDG 16).
- All of these are [[social-determinants-of-health]] → cross-cutting mental-health relevance.
