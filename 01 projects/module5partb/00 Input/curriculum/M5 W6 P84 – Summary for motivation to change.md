## Summary for motivation to change

## Key learning points

You have completed this section. Review each key learning point before you progress.

- Relapse occurs frequently in recovery and turns the change process into a cyclical process.
- It is important for people to maintain hope, even if they relapse during the change process.
- Clinicians need to maintain a balance between accepting that relapse will happen and not accepting that it is inevitable.
- The black box analogy is a way of visualizing events, analyzing why they caused relapse to happen, and exploring the feelings and emotions that were involved.
- The prospective hindsight technique allows people to examine plans and future events and consider how they will manage their recovery during them.
- People have different lived experiences of recovery, and different aspects of the journey will be important to them.
