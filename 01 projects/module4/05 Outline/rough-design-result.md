## Argumentation Chain
1. Anxiety is systemic, not individual → individual-only treatment insufficient
2. Family dynamics sustain anxiety → family-inclusive interventions needed
3. Digital environment amplifies anxiety in neurobiologically vulnerable adolescents → societal-level response needed
4. Both systems interact → stepped, coordinated care across levels is the evidence-based answer
