## Key usable claims
1. Diagnosed anxiety incidence in UK under-20s rose with an **incidence rate ratio of 3.51** (2003–2018) — a ~3.5-fold rise.
2. Authors note diagnostic change/reduced stigma cannot be fully ruled out, but a true increase is plausible.
