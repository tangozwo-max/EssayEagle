**Evidence tier:** Book chapter / review — **Tier T3 (review)** · **PDF:** full-pdf · **DOI:** 10.1093/oso/9780195314373.003.0007

## Key usable claims
1. Adolescence is a sensitive window of heightened neural plasticity in which experience — including therapeutic experience — shapes circuits that are not yet fixed.
2. The same immaturity that confers vulnerability to anxiety also confers the capacity for change.
