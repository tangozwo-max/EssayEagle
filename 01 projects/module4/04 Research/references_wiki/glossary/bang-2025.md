## Key usable claims
1. Anxiety-disorder incidence in **diagnosed specialist care** rose sharply in Norwegian youth, **especially adolescent girls**, 2010–2022.
2. The trends **largely predate COVID**, and because the rise is in **specialist care** (not community screening), it is **hard to explain purely by reduced stigma**.
3. Adds a Nordic registry data point to the genuine-incidence-rise case.

## Main limitation
Conference abstract only (abbreviated methods, not full peer review); specialist-care diagnoses still partly reflect referral/access patterns.
