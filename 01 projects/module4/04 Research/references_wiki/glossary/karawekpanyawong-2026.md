## Key usable claims
1. Moderate anxiolytic effect with good tolerability — but a **markedly elevated risk of suicidal ideation** (RR ~3.5).
2. Supports reserving pharmacotherapy for moderate-to-severe or treatment-refractory cases under monitoring.

## Cite for (essay relevance)
§2 status quo — the evaluative safety caution on SSRIs that confines them within stepped care.
