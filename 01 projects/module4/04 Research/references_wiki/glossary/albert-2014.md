## Key usable claims
1. Decreased serotonergic activity is implicated in both anxiety and depression, and antidepressants act by directly or indirectly **increasing the long-term activity of the serotonin system**.
2. 5-HT1A heteroreceptors are expressed post-synaptically in the **prefrontal cortex, amygdala and hippocampus**, where serotonin mediates its effects on **fear, anxiety, stress and cognition** — i.e. the same fronto-limbic threat circuitry implicated in childhood anxiety.
3. The 5-HT1A autoreceptor regulates the "gain" of the serotonin system; the balance of pre- vs post-synaptic 5-HT1A signalling shapes anxiety- vs depression-like phenotypes.
