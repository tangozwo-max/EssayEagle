## Key learning points

You have completed this section. Review each key learning point before you progress.

- Most mental health disorders arise before adulthood, and early interventions can prevent some of these.
- A continuum of need is a framework that organizes interventions along a spectrum of the needs of families and individuals.
- Mental health promotion involves proactive measures to enhance well-being and resilience to optimize positive mental health.
- Prevention aims to minimize poor mental health by addressing risk factors.
- Mental health screening aims to identify children and young people who may need further psychological support and intervention.
- Screening tools should be appropriate for the age group and context within which the screening is being carried out.
- There are ethical challenges around the use of screening tools, and these should be considered before they are utilized.
