## Key learning points

You have completed this section. Review each key learning point before you progress.

- The most common mental health disorders that initially present during childhood include depression, anxiety, and behavioral disorders.
- The standard clinical approach to mental health is a process that includes assessment, formulation, intervention, and evaluation.
- A formulation is a structured narrative that uses psychological theory to understand and make sense of an individual’s difficulties, their development, and the social, cultural, and relational factors influencing their current situation.
- The 5Ps model of formulation is a cognitive behavioral framework that examines an individual’s difficulties through five factors: presenting issues, predisposing factors, precipitating factors, perpetuating factors, and protective factors.
- Many cultural factors affect how mental health is perceived and managed, including parenting practices and expectations around independence and autonomy, behavior, and development.
