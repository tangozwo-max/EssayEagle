## Key learning points

You have completed this section. Review each key learning point before you progress.

- Developmental milestones refer to the skills and abilities that most children typically achieve by specific ages.
- Developmental milestones provide important information for mental health care and enable practitioners to adjust assessment methods, consider specific information to include in assessments, and refine treatment goals appropriately.
- These milestones cover various aspects of development, including physical, communication, cognitive, social, and emotional domains.
- Monitoring children's developmental progress and identifying potential delays early enable early intervention to be provided.
- The COVID-19 pandemic has disrupted developmental milestones for many children, affecting social interactions, learning environments, and routine healthcare visits.
