## Ethical dilemmas

Earlier in this course, you learned about some of the ethical challenges involved in providing mental health care.

This week you will consider some of the ethical considerations that relate more specifically to young people.

These include:

- confidentiality and disclosure
- informed consent
- autonomy, capacity, and competency
- best interests of the child
- mandatory reporting
- cultural and social sensitivity

If needed, refresh your knowledge of [ethics in mental healthcare](https://learn.iheed.org/courses/758/modules/items/356847).

> [!-info] -info
> **Note:** As with other topics discussed this week, the ethical challenges you may encounter will vary significantly depending on your setting. The topics selected for this learning module aim to provide a broad spectrum of ethical challenges to enhance your understanding and preparedness.
